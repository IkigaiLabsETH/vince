# Naval's 13 Career-Audit Prompts

Naval (the agent) can run these 13 prompts. Ask by name or trigger phrase. Paste your context (background, opportunity, decision, skills, etc.) in the same message.

| # | Prompt | When to use | Trigger phrases |
|---|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Specific Knowledge Audit** | Find what only you can do; can't be trained, from experience, do for free, where you're authentic | specific knowledge audit, specific knowledge, what only I can do, career audit naval |
| 2 | **Leverage Identification** | Which leverage (code, media, labor, capital) do you have? How to acquire in 6 months? Rank by ease to scale | leverage identification, which leverage, code media labor capital, my leverage |
| 3 | **Long-Term Games** | Is this opportunity a long-term game with long-term people? What compounds in 5 years? | long term games, opportunity validator, compound 5 years |
| 4 | **Productize Yourself** | 12-month roadmap: build (1-3), sell (4-6), scale (7-9), outcome (10-12). Sell + build = unstoppable | productize myself, sell and build, 12 month roadmap |
| 5 | **Accountability vs Authenticity** | Authentic or for show? Willing to be publicly accountable? What if no one was watching? | accountability authenticity, authentic or status, naval test decision |
| 6 | **Reading for Understanding** | Extract 3 mental models from a book, apply to your problem, should you finish, what to read next | reading for understanding, mental models in this book, should I finish this book |
| 7 | **Expected Value** | Best case, worst case, probability per option. Highest EV or reject all. If you can't decide, the answer is no | expected value, EV decision, best case worst case probability |
| 8 | **Credibility vs Status** | Audit last 6 months: building credibility or chasing status? What to kill, what to double down on | credibility vs status, building credibility, status audit |
| 9 | **Wealth Creation vs Capture** | Creating new value (positive sum) or capturing existing (zero sum)? If pure capture, what would creation look like? | wealth creation capture, positive sum zero sum |
| 10 | **Escape Competition** | Find 2-3 skill intersections: top 10% each, unique combo, demand. What would you call yourself? | escape competition, skill intersection, monopoly |
| 11 | **Retirement Cope** | Retirement = today is complete in itself. Love it or leading somewhere? Would you do it if wealthy? | retirement cope, retirement test, would I do this if wealthy |
| 12 | **Build in Public** | Should you build publicly? Accountability if it fails, leverage from being public. What to share weekly | build in public, accountability leverage, share publicly |
| 13 | **Narrow Niche** | Narrow until you're the obvious choice. "I'm the only person who [X] for [Y]." | narrow niche, niche down, obvious choice |

Example: "Run the specific knowledge audit. About me: 10 years fintech, design + code, love systems."

Or: "Expected value: stay at job vs quit and freelance. Best/worst/probability for each."
