# nav.al Essay Themes — Intent-to-Essay Map

When a user runs one of the 13 career-audit prompts or asks about a core theme, cite the relevant nav.al essays. Point them to https://nav.al/archive.

## 13 prompts → essays

**1. Specific Knowledge Audit**
- Find Your Specific Knowledge Through Action
- Specific Knowledge Is Highly Creative or Technical
- Arm Yourself With Specific Knowledge
- *When to cite:* User asks what only they can do, or runs the specific knowledge audit.

**2. Leverage Identification**
- Labor and Capital Are Old Leverage
- Product and Media are New Leverage
- Product Leverage is Egalitarian
- Pick a Business Model With Leverage
- *When to cite:* User asks which leverage they have (code, media, labor, capital) or how to scale.

**3. Long-Term Games**
- Play Long-term Games With Long-term People
- Turn Short-Term Games Into Long-Term Games
- Compounding Relationships Make Life Easier
- *When to cite:* User evaluates an opportunity for compounding or long-term people.

**4. Productize Yourself**
- Productize Yourself
- Learn to Sell, Learn to Build
- *When to cite:* User asks how to productize themselves or build a 12-month roadmap.

**5. Accountability vs Authenticity**
- Accountability Means Letting People Criticize You
- Take Accountability to Earn Equity
- Embrace Accountability to Get Leverage
- Escape Competition Through Authenticity
- *When to cite:* User asks whether a move is authentic or for show, or about public accountability.

**6. Reading for Understanding**
- Read What You Love Until You Love to Read
- Read the Best 100 Books Over and Over Again
- Most Books Should Be Skimmed, A Few Should Be Devoured
- The Best Authors Respect the Reader's Time
- *When to cite:* User asks how to read for understanding, which book to finish, or what to read next.

**7. Expected Value / Judgment**
- Judgment Is the Decisive Skill
- Kelly Criterion: Avoid Ruin
- *When to cite:* User runs the expected-value prompt or asks how to decide between options.

**8. Credibility vs Status**
- Don't Rely on Credibility Stamps
- *When to cite:* User audits last 6 months for credibility vs status chasing.

**9. Wealth Creation vs Capture**
- Make Abundance for the World
- Give Society What It Doesn't Know How to Get
- Seek Wealth, Not Money or Status
- *When to cite:* User asks whether an opportunity creates or captures wealth, or positive vs zero sum.

**10. Escape Competition**
- Escape Competition Through Authenticity
- Keep Redefining What You Do
- *When to cite:* User asks how to find a unique combination of skills or escape competition.

**11. Retirement / Happiness**
- Happiness Is a Skill You Can Develop
- Desire Is a Contract You Make to Be Unhappy
- The Path to Peace Is Truth
- Work the Least for It
- When You Truly Work for Yourself
- Happiness Is Peace in Motion
- *When to cite:* User runs the retirement test or asks about happiness, desire, or doing work they’d do if wealthy.

**12. Build in Public**
- Accountability Means Letting People Criticize You
- Take Accountability to Earn Equity
- *When to cite:* User asks whether to build publicly or what to share weekly.

**13. Narrow Niche**
- Give Society What It Doesn't Know How to Get
- Keep Redefining What You Do
- *When to cite:* User asks how to narrow positioning until they’re the obvious choice.
