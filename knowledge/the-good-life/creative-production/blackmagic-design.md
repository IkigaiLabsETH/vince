# Blackmagic Design

## Overview

Blackmagic Design creates professional video and film equipment used in Hollywood productions. Their ecosystem spans cameras, capture devices, switchers, and post-production tools, all designed for cinema-quality results at accessible price points.

## Camera Lineup

### URSA Cine Series
- **URSA Cine 12K LF**: Full-frame 12K sensor, 16 stops dynamic range
- **URSA Cine 17K 65mm**: Largest sensor for ultimate image quality
- **URSA Cine Immersive**: Stereoscopic 3D for Apple Vision Pro content
- Features: 8TB cloud storage module, interchangeable lens mounts

### PYXIS Series
- **PYXIS 6K**: Full-frame 36x24mm sensor, L-mount
- **PYXIS 12K**: Revolutionary RGBW sensor, 16 stops dynamic range
- Compact cube design for versatile mounting
- CFexpress recording with proxy files

### Pocket Cinema Camera
- **Pocket 4K**: Micro Four Thirds mount, 13 stops dynamic range
- **Pocket 6K G2**: Super 35 EF mount, tilting LCD
- **Pocket 6K Pro**: Built-in ND filters, optional EVF
- Affordable cinema cameras with professional features

### Studio Cameras
- **Studio Camera 4K Plus G2**: Live production with 7" LCD
- **Studio Camera 6K Pro**: Larger sensor, remote ND control
- Built-in color corrector, USB recording

## Recording Formats

### Blackmagic RAW
- Visually lossless compression
- CPU and GPU accelerated
- Full sensor data preservation
- Smaller files than ProRes RAW
- 12-bit precision

### ProRes Support
- Apple ProRes 422/4444
- Industry-standard delivery format
- Wide compatibility

## Key Technologies

### Color Science
- 5th generation color science
- Wide gamut support
- Film-like image quality
- Accurate skin tones

### Dynamic Range
- Up to 16 stops on cinema cameras
- HDR workflow support
- Clean shadows and highlights

### Resolution
- 4K, 6K, 8K, 12K, 17K options
- Super-sampling for cleaner output
- Crop modes for different needs

## Ecosystem Integration
