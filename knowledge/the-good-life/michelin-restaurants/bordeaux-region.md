## Le Saint-James (Bouliac)

**5-Star Relais & Châteaux | Jean Nouvel Architecture**

- **Address**: 3 Place Camille Hostein, 33270 Bouliac
- **Website**: https://www.saintjames-bouliac.com/
- **Phone**: +33 5 57 97 06 00
- **Architect**: Jean Nouvel (interior refresh by Laurent Maugoust)

**History**:

- 18th-century longère → 1930s mondain bar → 1989 Jean Nouvel transformation
- Renovated 2024, respecting Nouvel's vision

**Restaurants**:

- **Restaurant Le Saint-James** - gastronomic
- **Orama** - bistronomic
- **Côté Cours** - cooking school

**Hotel**:

- Chambre Classique, Deluxe, Prestige
- Suite Junior, Jean Nouvel, Horizon, Eden, Céleste

**Views**: Panoramic over Garonne valley and Bordeaux skyline

**Note**: Reopening May 14, 2026

**Best For**: Architecture lovers, Bordeaux panorama, Relais & Châteaux experience
