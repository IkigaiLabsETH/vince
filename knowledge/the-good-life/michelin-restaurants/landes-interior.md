# Landes Interior Fine Dining

> **Region**: Magescq, Mont-de-Marsan, St-Paul-lès-Dax, Uza
> **Vibe**: Gascon tradition, family estates, countryside charm

---


## Villa Mirasol (Mont-de-Marsan)

**1-Star Michelin + Bib Gourmand Bistrot**

- **Address**: 2 Boulevard Ferdinand de Candau, 40000 Mont-de-Marsan
- **Website**: https://www.villamirasol.fr/
- **Phone**: +33 5 58 44 14 14
- **Style**: Belle Époque mansion (1912), 4* hotel

**Chef**: Philippe Lagraula
- Ex-Michel Troisgros, Michel Bras, Nicolas Le Bec
- Former 1* at "Une cuisine en ville" (Dax)
- Latin American influences

**Restaurants**:
- **Table Mirasol** (1* since 2023) - refined Southwest + travel inspirations
- **Bistrot 1912** (Bib Gourmand since 2022) - spontaneous, convivial

**Hotel**: 7 rooms, gardens on Trois Rivières, Musée Despiau-Wlérick sculptures

**Best For**: Art + gastronomy, Belle Époque charm, business stays

For current opening hours call +33 5 58 44 14 14 or see villamirasol.fr.

---

## Moulin de Poustagnacq (St-Paul-lès-Dax)

**Gascon Classic by the Lake**

- **Address**: 40990 St-Paul-lès-Dax
- **Website**: https://poustagnacq.fr/
- **Phone**: +33 5 58 91 31 03
- **Chef**: Thierry Berthelier (30+ years)

**Experience**:
- Historic mill in pine forest
- Lakeside terrace dining
- 200+ wine references
- Classic French with Gascon accent
- Celebrated by Michelin and Gault & Millau
- Collège Culinaire de France member

**Signature**: Jabugo Pata Negra, foie gras with house fig compote

**Best For**: Traditional Gascon, romantic lakeside setting

---

## La Table du Marensin (Uza)

**Chef's Table at Domaine d'Uza**

- **Address**: 115 Rue de Castets, 40170 Uza
- **Website**: https://domaineduza.fr/la-table-du-marensin/
- **Phone**: Contact via website
- **Style**: Modern local cuisine, author-driven

**Experience**:
- Renovated engineer's lodge from old forge
- 30 covers inside + 30 on terrace
- Own herb garden, edible flowers
- 200+ wine references
- Recognized by Michelin and Gault & Millau
- Collège Culinaire de France member

**Also at Domaine**: La Forge (simpler cuisine), pâtisseries

**Note**: Winter hours - closed Mon-Tue, Wed evening, Sun evening

**Best For**: Inventive local cuisine, hidden gem discovery
