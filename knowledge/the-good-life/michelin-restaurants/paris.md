## Booking Strategy

### 3-Star
- 2-3 months advance minimum
- L'Ambroisie: Call only, extremely limited
- Most offer online booking now
- Lunch often easier

### Popular 1-Star
- Septime: Book exactly when released
- 3-4 weeks for most others
- Weekday lunch best availability

### Bistronomy
- Week ahead typically fine
- Walk-in possible off-peak

## Value Strategies

### Lunch Menus
- Most starred offer set lunch
- Often 50-70% of dinner
- Shorter but same kitchen

### Hotel Restaurants
- Often overlooked
- Excellent quality
- Easier reservations
