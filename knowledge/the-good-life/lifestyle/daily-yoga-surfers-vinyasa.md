# Daily Yoga for Surfers: Vinyasa + Surfer Yoga

Summary for Kelly's recommendations.

## Household practice

- **Daily vinyasa (wife):** 20–30 min morning vinyasa; consistency and breath-movement connection. Use **Yoga with Kassandra** (YouTube) for flows—morning energy, full body vinyasa, yin for recovery.
- **Surfer yoga (pre/post surf):** Hip openers, shoulder mobility, balance.
  - **Pre-surf (≈10 min):** cat-cow, thread the needle, hip circles, shoulder rolls, tree/eagle.
  - **Post-surf (≈20 min):** pigeon, lizard, shoulder stretches (eagle arms, cow face arms).

## Yoga with Kassandra

- **Channel:** https://www.youtube.com/@yogawithkassandra
- **Use for:** Morning flows (15–30 min), full body vinyasa, yin for recovery, stress relief. Search her playlists for "morning yoga", "vinyasa flow", "yin yoga", "10 min", "20 min" to match session length.

## References

- **yoga-practice.md:** Katarina daily vinyasa (Kassandra), Yves surfer yoga & 50+ mobility.

When recommending yoga: prefer vinyasa for daily practice; for surf days recommend **10 min pre-surf** warm-up and **20 min post-surf** recovery from yoga-practice.
