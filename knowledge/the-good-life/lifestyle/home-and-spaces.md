# Home & Spaces

## The Property — Landes, SW France

Between Bordeaux (~2h) and Biarritz (~45 min). Hossegor is the local hub.

---

## Wine Cellar

**100 sqm dedicated cellar** + a wine fridge for ready-to-drink rotation.

**White Burgundy** is the house white — the go-to, always stocked:
- Chablis (crisp, mineral, the aperitif default)
- Meursault, Puligny-Montrachet, Chassagne-Montrachet when available
- Saint-Véran, Mâcon-Villages for everyday

**Sancerre** — Loire Sauvignon Blanc. Clean, citrus, great with seafood and lighter meals.

**Reds — Bordeaux Left & Right Bank:**
- **Margaux** — the favorite appellation. Elegant, perfumed, silky. Also the cat's name.
- **Pauillac** — power and structure (Mouton, Lafite, Lynch-Bages)
- **Saint-Julien** — balanced, consistent (Léoville-Las Cases, Ducru-Beaucaillou)
- **Saint-Estèphe** — firm, earthy (Cos d'Estournel, Montrose)
- **Pomerol** — Right Bank richness (Pétrus, Le Pin, L'Évangile)
- **Saint-Émilion** — Right Bank warmth (Cheval Blanc, Ausone, Figeac)
- **Pessac-Léognan** — gravelly elegance, both red and white (Haut-Brion, Domaine de Chevalier, Château Olivier)

**Champagne** — popped for midweek celebrations. Not saved for occasions — if it's Wednesday and something good happened, that's reason enough.

Kelly should reference the cellar naturally: "you've got that Margaux in the cellar — tonight's the night" or "grab a Chablis from the fridge, it's perfect for this."
