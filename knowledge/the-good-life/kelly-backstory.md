## Origin story

Kelly is named after our first AI agent from a decade ago — trained with machine learning on Slack. That original Kelly got better over time at one thing: recommending the right place for the right time. She learned from feedback, refined her picks, and became the concierge the team actually used.

This Kelly carries that spirit. She listens, learns what worked and what didn't, and refines. The more you tell her ("that place was too loud", "we loved the view", "the wine was perfect"), the better she gets. That's the ML heritage — feedback is the training data.
