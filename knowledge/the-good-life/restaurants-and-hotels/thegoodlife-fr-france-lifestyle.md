## South of France Hotels (TGL Top 15)

### Provence & Luberon
- **La Bastide, Airelles, Gordes** — Palace. 40 rooms. 16th century. Toile de Jouy. Jean-François Piège at Clover Gordes. Pierre Marty (Ducasse-trained) at La Table. New: Beefar pop-up (street food + Green Egg). Airelles Spa by Guerlain. From €1,150/night low season, €1,650 high. airelles.com

### Saint-Tropez & Côte d'Azur
- **Byblos, Saint-Tropez** — Icon since 1960s. Les Caves du Roy nightclub. Laura Gonzalez renovation. New: Zuma Japanese restaurant, rooftop bar, Porsche 1970 customized (for sale). From €600/night. byblos.com
- **COMO Le Beauvallon** — Reopening April 2026. Dominates Gulf of Saint-Tropez. Original 1914. Gardens, pool, restaurant, beach club. comohotels.com
- **Château de Théoule** — Maison Millésime. Built 1630 (former soap factory). 44 rooms sea view. Marie-Christine Mecoen design. Beach, pool, rooftop, spa. 55 Av. de Lérins, Théoule-sur-Mer. chateau-de-theoule.com

### Camargue
- **Mas de Peint, Arles** — 5★ Teritoria. 500 hectares in natural park. Gardian culture (bull herding). Lucile Bon design. Beach 10km. From €290/night. teritoria.com
- **Les Bains Gardians** — Near Saintes-Maries-de-la-Mer. Gardian cabins redesigned by Hauvette & Madani.

### Monaco
- **Monte-Carlo Beach** — SBM. Centenaire. Dorothée Delaye redesign. Marcel Ravin (starred chef). Sea Satin at Maona (Mykonos-style). Jondal (Ibiza-style ultraluxe). Olympic pool, Nikki Beach (10th anniversary), Clarins spa. From €1,057/night. montecarlosbm.com
- **Fairmont Monte Carlo** — 596 rooms. F1 hairpin view. Nobu restaurant. Amù Mediterranean. Nikki Beach rooftop. From €289/night low, €469 high. fairmont-montecarlo.com

### Corsica
- **Mylos, Cargèse** — Dorothée Meilichzon design. 34 rooms in former mill ruins. Earth construction + vegetalized roofs. Chef Pierre Géomine. From €150/night. mylos-hotel.com
- **Domaine Le Mouflon d'Or, Zonza** — Relais & Châteaux 5★. 20 rooms. Ancient building, newly renovated. Near Bavella needles. From €550/night high season. lemouflondor.com

### Hyères / Var
- **Lilou, Hyères** — 37 rooms in restored 1890 hotel. David Pirone / Haddou Dufourcq studio. Fig-scented. lilouhotel.fr
- **Zannier Île de Bendor** — Private island (Var), reopening spring 2026. 93 keys. Paul Ricard's original island. Spa, beach club, dive center, 8 restaurants. zannier.com

### Arcachon / Cap Ferret
- **Les Vagues, Arcachon** — First 5★ in Arcachon. Glass tower + renovated Arcachonnaise villa. Rooftop, spa. hotel-lesvagues-arcachon.com
- **Hôtel de la Plage, Cap Ferret** — Institution since 1860. 12 rooms, most with sea view. No TV, no AC, no minibar — back to essentials. Maison Faber Lascombes. hoteldelaplage-cap-ferret.fr

## Cars & Driving — TGL Picks

### Porsche 911 992.2 T-Hybrid (Dec 2025 review)
- First hybrid 911 ever. Two electric motors (at PDK gearbox + turbo)
- 541 hp, 610 Nm. 0-100 in 3.1s. Top speed 312 km/h
- "The response is truly instant — you must dose carefully"
- Spirit 70 edition: 1,500 units. Olive Neo paint, Pascha psychedelic interior. €247,200 (+ €70k malus)
- GTS base: from €180,600 (Cabriolet €194,800)
- Still max ecological penalty — the electric boost is for performance, not eco

### TGL Electric Car Picks 2025
1. **Alpine A290** — Car of the Year 2025. Based on R5. 220hp. Sporty, agile, daily-drivable. Fun over power.
2. **Tesla Model Y** — Refreshed. Improved quality/comfort. Top 3 global sales (all powertrains).
3. **Volvo EX90** — 7-seat luxury SUV. Air suspension. 580km range. 2.8 tonnes.
4. **Smart #5** — Family SUV. Brabus version: 646hp. Can sleep inside.
5. **DS NO 8** — French flagship crossover-berline. 750km range. Bicolor paint. Refined cabin.
