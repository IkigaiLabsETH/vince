# 2026 Hotel Openings — France

> Source: [The Good Life](https://thegoodlife.fr) · January 2026
> 17 new properties worth knowing about.

---

## Winter 2026

### Bus Palladium — Paris

- **Opens:** January 2026
- **The deal:** Studio KO transformed the legendary Pigalle rock venue into a 35-room hotel with a 200-seat concert hall still intact. The Magic Rock suite is the one to book.
- **Rooms:** 35
- **Design:** Studio KO

### Château La Commaraine — Pommard, Burgundy

- **Opens:** February 2026
- **The deal:** Burgundy's first 5-star hotel, sitting on 4 hectares of premier cru vineyards. 37 rooms. Chef Christophe Raoux runs the kitchen. You're sleeping in the vineyard, literally.
- **Rooms:** 37
- **Chef:** Christophe Raoux

---

## Early Spring 2026

### Les Sources de Vougeot — Burgundy

- **Opens:** March 2026
- **The deal:** Château de Gilly-lès-Cîteaux on the Côte de Nuits, reimagined with 49 rooms, a Caudalie Spa, and chef Julien Martin. Serious wine country, serious hotel.
- **Rooms:** 49
- **Spa:** Caudalie
- **Chef:** Julien Martin

---

## April 2026

### Bel Hôtel Oléron, MGallery — Île d'Oléron

- **Opens:** April 2026
- **The deal:** 102 rooms between ocean and pine forest. Pierre Gagnaire oversees the cuisine. Spa marin on-site. Atlantic coast done properly.
- **Rooms:** 102
- **Chef:** Pierre Gagnaire (cuisine direction)
- **Spa:** Marin

### Villa Florentine — Lyon

- **Opens:** April 2026
- **The deal:** Relais & Châteaux, housed in a 17th-century convent above Lyon. Joséphine Fossey led the renovation. Panoramic terraces overlooking the city. Lyon's best perch.
- **Rooms:** TBC
- **Collection:** Relais & Châteaux

### COMO Le Beauvallon — Saint-Tropez

- **Opens:** April 24, 2026
- **The deal:** Historic 1914 property, now under COMO Hotels. Gulf of Saint-Tropez views, private beach club. COMO's wellness pedigree meets the Riviera.
- **Collection:** COMO Hotels

---

## Late Spring 2026

### Cœur Marin — Les Sables-d'Olonne

- **Opens:** May 2026
- **The deal:** 42-room 4-star with a rooftop. Vendée coast, walkable to the Remblai. Smart pick for the Atlantic without the Biarritz price tag.
- **Rooms:** 42

### Zannier Île de Bendor — Var

- **Opens:** Spring 2026
- **The deal:** An entire private island off Bandol (formerly Paul Ricard's playground). 93 keys, 8 restaurants, dive center. This is the big swing of the year.
- **Rooms:** 93
- **Collection:** Zannier Hotels

### Hôtel La Bourdonnais — Paris 7e

- **Opens:** Spring 2026
- **The deal:** 53 rooms in the 7th, steps from the Eiffel Tower. Oscar Lucien Ono on design. Compact, well-located, beautifully done.
- **Rooms:** 53
- **Design:** Oscar Lucien Ono

---

## Summer 2026

### La Maison Le Chevreuil — Meursault, Burgundy

- **Opens:** June 2026
- **The deal:** 10-room wine-themed maison in the heart of Meursault. Intimate, focused, for people who take white Burgundy seriously.
- **Rooms:** 10

### Relais d'Amboise — Loire Valley

- **Opens:** Summer 2026
- **The deal:** First 5-star in Amboise. Managed by Marugal. Rooftop views of the château, spa carved into troglodyte caves. Loire luxury finally has an anchor.
- **Collection:** Marugal

### Château de Cîteaux — Burgundy

- **Opens:** Mid 2026
- **The deal:** Fontenille Collection takes on a property with 12th-century Cistercian caves. 36 rooms. History you can touch.
- **Rooms:** 36
- **Collection:** Fontenille

---

## Autumn–Winter 2026

### Hôtel Saint-Roch — Courchevel 1850

- **Opens:** Late 2026
- **The deal:** Maisons Pariente (the group behind Lou Pinet and Le Coucou) opens in Courchevel 1850. Expect the same refined, personal style at altitude.
- **Collection:** Maisons Pariente

### L'Ile Seguin — Boulogne-Billancourt

- **Opens:** November 2026
- **The deal:** Sarah Lavoine–designed hotel on the Seine island that houses La Seine Musicale. Cultural hub location, river views, 15 minutes from central Paris.
- **Design:** Sarah Lavoine

### Banke Opéra Paris — Paris 9e

- **Opens:** Late 2026
- **The deal:** Radisson Collection, 90 rooms in a Belle Époque building near Opéra Garnier. Grand Parisian bones, modern execution.
- **Rooms:** 90
- **Collection:** Radisson Collection

### Salvia — Paris 5e

- **Opens:** Winter 2026
- **The deal:** Latin Quarter literary hotel with a coffee shop and œnophile restaurant. For the bookish, wine-drinking Paris visitor.

### La Maregagne — Vexin

- **Opens:** Late 2026
- **The deal:** Farm-hotel on 110 hectares in the Vexin. Agritourism meets luxury. An hour from Paris, a world away.
