# The Good Life Magazine — Restaurant & Hotel Picks

> Source: The Good Life magazine (thegoodlife.fr). All places verified from published articles. Drive times from Hossegor.

**Drive times from Hossegor:**

- Biarritz: ~45 min
- San Sebastián: ~1h15
- Bordeaux: ~2h

---

## Hossegor & Landes

### Hotels

- **Les Hortensias du Lac** — 5-star, Domaine de Fontenille group. Lakeside, typical Landaise bâtisse. 10 min bike to ocean. 1578 avenue du Tour du Lac, Hossegor. [hortensias-du-lac.com](https://hortensias-du-lac.com)
- **Villa Seren** — 4-star eco-responsible. Former Villa Clovis (oldest house on lake, 1898). Double façade, solar panels, heated covered pool. 1111 Av. du Touring Club, Hossegor. [villaseren.fr](https://villaseren.fr)
- **Iconic House Les Bords du Lac** — Private luxury rental (6 suites, 380m²). Chef on demand, helicopter, concierge. Cinema room, heated pool on lake.

### Restaurants

- **Bistro Balnéaire** — Lakeside, versatile menu (moules au Roquefort, burgers, chirashi). Multiple terraces. 1830 Av. du Touring Club. [lebistrobalneaire.fr](https://lebistrobalneaire.fr)
- **Spot Palace** — Zone Pedebert. Coolest address in Hossegor per TGL. Smash burgers, bobun végétarien. No reservations. 251 Av. des Menuisiers. [spotpalace.fr](https://spotpalace.fr)
- **La Nord** — Place des Landais revival. Local produce + world flavors, natural wines. 39 Rue des Landais. [lanordhossegor.com](https://lanordhossegor.com)
- **Lake House** — Near oyster cabins. Asian-leaning (bao sandwiches, Asian soups, fresh salads). 2467 avenue du TCF Plage du Rey. Tel: 05 58 57 46 80

## Biarritz & Pays Basque

### Hotels

- **Regina Experimental** — Experimental Group. Centenaire Art Deco building, 72 rooms. Designed by Dorothée Meilichzon. Frenchie Biarritz restaurant (Grégory Marchand). Spa (Susanne Kaufmann + Alaena). 52 avenue de l'Impératrice. [reginaexperimental.com](https://reginaexperimental.com)
- **Hôtel de la Plage** — Overlooking Port-Vieux crique. Reprise by Olivier Granet-Sottis. 3 Esp. du Port Vieux.

### Restaurants

- **Frenchie Biarritz** (at Regina) — Grégory Marchand (starred Paris chef). Gougères à l'Ossau-Iraty, raviolis chèvre fumé & piquillios, cochon noir gascon en croûte de sel.
- **Carlos** — Côte des Basques institution. Bellota, carpaccio courgettes, riz au homard bleu. DJ evenings. 7 boulevard du Prince-de-Galles. Tel: 06 37 99 85 79. [carlosbiarritz.com](https://carlosbiarritz.com)
- **Club Sandwich** — Chef Elsa Rampazzo. Natural wines, vinyl nights. 24 Rue d'Espagne.
- **Petit Grill Basque** (Saint-Jean-de-Luz) — Chef Iñaki Aizpitarte (ex-Chateaubriant Paris). Old institution renovated. Tripes de morue, veau rosé beurre pimenté, riz aux carabineros. Bilingual Basque-French menu. 4 rue Saint-Jacques.
- **Blue Cargo** (Bidart) — Beach bar since 1991. Plage d'Ibarritz. Cocktails, DJ, covered terrace. 2 avenue du Lac. [bluecargo.fr](https://bluecargo.fr)
- **Hétéroclito** (Guéthary) — "Comptoir sur l'océan" above Parlementia wave. Plancha seafood. Chemin de la plage. [heteroclito.fr](https://heteroclito.fr)
- **Ilunabar** (Guéthary) — Cabane by Suhubiette twins. Sunset cocktails overlooking Parlementia. 50 promenade de la Plage.
- **Alaïa** (Ciboure/Socoa) — Only restaurant directly on Socoa beach. Chef Fabrice Naacke (ex-Paris). Ultra-local: thon de Saint-Jean, turbot espagnol, chipirons only in season (September). Bio wines from across the border. Plage de Socoa. Tel: 05 59 47 43 79. [restaurantalaia.com](https://restaurantalaia.com)

## San Sebastián (~1h15 from Hossegor)

### Hotels

- **Hotel Maria Cristina** — Most luxurious. Opened 1912. Starwood/Luxury Collection. Cocktails at Bar Dry (Javier de las Muelas). Cooking school. Paseo Republica Argentina, 4. Tel: +34 943 437 600
- **Villa Soro** — 25 rooms in late-19th-century mansion. Heights of Gros. No restaurant. Av. de Ategorrieta, 61. Tel: +34 943 297 970
- **Hotel de Londres y de Inglaterra** — Historic palace on La Concha bay. Renovated 2013 + rooftop floor. Pool + gym. Zubieta, 2. Tel: +34 943 440 770
- **Hotel Astoria7** — Cinema-themed. Quiet (20 min walk from center). Sagrada Familia, 1. Tel: +34 943 445 000

### Restaurants — Fine Dining

- **Arzak** — 3★ Michelin since 1989. Elena Arzak (4th generation). Noble products + exotic flavors. Flavor lab with 1,500 ingredients. Alcalde J. Elosegi, 273. Tel: +34 943 278 465. [arzak.es](https://arzak.es)
- **Akelarre** — 3★ Michelin. Chef Pedro Subijana. Pioneer of nueva cocina vasca. Gulf views. Also hotel + Espazio Oteiza terrace for lighter bites. Padre Orcolaga, 56. Tel: +34 943 311 208. [akelarre.net](https://akelarre.net)
- **Mugaritz** — 2★ Michelin. Chef Andoni Luis Aduriz. (Referenced via his spinoff Topa)

### Restaurants — Casual / Pintxos

- **Topa Sukaldería** — Andoni Luis Aduriz's Latin American-Basque fusion. Tiradito d'anchois, profiteroles au mole, tacos maïs-millet. Calle Aguirre Miramón, 7. Tel: +34 943 569 143
- **Bodegón Alejandro** — Impeccable traditional revisited. Menu dégustation €46. Joues de morue au txakoli. Tarte aux pommes thym-romarin. Calle Fermín Calbetón, 4. Tel: +34 943 427 158
- **Geralds Bar** — Australian Gerald Diffey (ex-Melbourne). Market cuisine, silver cutlery, wine bar. Iparragirre, 13. Tel: +34 943 083 001
- **Casa Urola** — Pintxos downstairs (order from blackboard), restaurant upstairs. Grilled meats, seasonal fish. Fermín Calbetón, 20. Tel: +34 943 441 371

### Pintxos Bars — Essential Crawl

- **La Cuchara de San Telmo** — Tiny, queues at opening. Joue de bœuf, foie gras, poulpe, risotto. Arrive early. Calle 31 de Agosto, 28. Tel: +34 943 435 446
- **Casa Valles** — Since 3 generations. Birthplace of the Gilda pintxo. Best jamón ibérico (bracelet noir). Reyes Católicos, 10. Tel: +34 943 452 210
- **Bar Martínez** — Fried specialties, morue confite. Calle 31 de Agosto, 13. Tel: +34 943 424 965
- **Hidalgo 56** — "Volcan de boudin" (black pudding + egg yolk + potato purée). Paseo Colón, 15. Tel: +34 943 279 654

### Wine

- **Goñi Ardoteka** — Best wine shop. Three brothers. Ground floor whites, basement reds. All Spanish regions + French selection + bio/small producers. Calle Aldamar, 3. Tel: +33 943 211 597
