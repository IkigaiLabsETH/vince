## Les vins rouges du Sud-Ouest (Red Wines)

### Cahors (Malbec / Cot)

- **Domaine Le Sid — Matthieu Cosse — « Le Sid » 2003** — **Cahors** (Malbec-led; "black wine of Cahors"). Matthieu Cosse is a leading organic producer. 2003 mature; dark fruit, structure. Decant 30–45 min; 16–18 °C; lamb, duck, game.

### Irouléguy (Basque Country)

- **Domaine Arretxea — « Cuvée Haitza » 2010** — **Irouléguy** (Pyrénées-Atlantiques); Tannat, Cabernet Franc. Arretxea is a reference estate. Cuvée Haitza: structured, red fruit, spice. Decant 30 min; 16–18 °C; lamb, axoa, Basque cuisine.
- **Domaine Arretxea — Hegoxuri 2012** — Irouléguy cuvée Hegoxuri; same estate. 2012 drinking now; 16–18 °C; lamb, charcuterie.

### Madiran (Tannat)

- **Château Bouscassé 2009** — **Madiran** (Alain Brumont estate); Tannat-led, powerful, age-worthy. 2009 in a good window; decant 45–60 min; 16–18 °C; lamb, daube, grilled meat.
- **Château Montus 2009** — **Madiran** (Alain Brumont flagship); Tannat-led, dense, long-lived. 2009 in a good window; decant 45–60 min; 16–18 °C; game, steak.

## Côtes de Gascogne (dry white)

- **Domaine Chiroulet — La Côte d’Heux — Sec 2013** — **Côtes de Gascogne** dry white (Colombard, Ugni blanc, etc.). Chiroulet is a quality estate. Sec = dry; fresh, citrus, apéritif or light fish. 8–10 °C; oysters, seafood, apéritif.
