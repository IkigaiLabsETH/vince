# High-Scoring French Wines (90+ Parker / Wine Advocate)

## How to use this note

**Scores (e.g. 90+ Parker points, Wine Advocate, Vinous)** vary **by vintage and by specific cuvée**. There is no single fixed list of "every French wine with 90+ points" — vintages are re-scored, new wines appear, and critics differ. Kelly should:

1. **Use this note** for **categories and representative names** that **typically** score 90+ in strong vintages.
2. For **exact scores** for a **specific wine and vintage**, use **WEB_SEARCH** (e.g. "Château X 2019 Wine Advocate score") or recommend the user check Wine Advocate, Vinous, or Decanter.
3. **Never invent** a score; if unsure, say "top vintages often 90+" or "check Wine Advocate for this vintage."

## Categories that routinely reach 90+

### Bordeaux (Left Bank)

- **First growths (1855):** Lafite, Latour, Margaux, Haut-Brion, Mouton — **95–100** in top vintages.
- **Pauillac:** Pichon Comtesse, Pichon Baron, Lynch-Bages, Pontet-Canet — **90–96+**.
- **Saint-Julien:** Léoville Las Cases, Ducru-Beaucaillou, Léoville Barton, Gruaud Larose, Beychevelle — **90–95+**.
- **Saint-Estèphe:** Cos d'Estournel, Montrose, Calon-Ségur — **90–98+**.
- **Margaux:** Château Margaux, Palmer, Rauzan-Ségla, Brane-Cantenac — **90–98+**.
- **Pessac-Léognan / Graves:** Haut-Brion, La Mission Haut-Brion, Domaine de Chevalier, Smith Haut Lafitte, Pape-Clément, Haut-Bailly — **90–100** (red and white for classed whites).

### Bordeaux (Right Bank)

- **Saint-Émilion:** Cheval Blanc, Ausone, Figeac, Pavie, Angelus, Canon — **90–98+**.
- **Pomerol:** Pétrus, Le Pin, Lafleur, Vieux Château Certan, L'Evangile, La Conseillante, Trotanoy — **90–100**.

### Burgundy

- **Grand cru and top premier cru** (Côte de Nuits and Côte de Beaune): **90–98+** in top vintages (e.g. Chambertin, Musigny, Montrachet, Corton-Charlemagne).
- **Chablis grand cru** (e.g. Raveneau, Dauvissat, Fèvre Les Clos): **90–95+**.
- **Top domaines** (Leroy, DRC, Coche-Dury, Leflaive, Ramonet, etc.): **90–100** for top cuvées.

### Rhône

- **Hermitage** (red and white): Chave, Jaboulet La Chapelle, Sorrel, etc. — **90–98+**.
- **Côte-Rôtie:** Guigal La Landonne / La Mouline / La Turque, Rostaing, Jamet — **90–98+**.
- **Châteauneuf-du-Pape:** Beaucastel, Rayas, Vieux Télégraphe, Clos des Papes — **90–95+**.
- **Condrieu:** Cuilleron, Vernay, Perret, Villard, Guigal La Doriane — **90–95+**.

### Loire

- **Sancerre / Pouilly-Fumé (top cuvées):** Cotat, Mellot, Vatan, Vacheron, Dagueneau — **90–95+**.

### Champagne

- **Prestige cuvées:** Dom Pérignon, Krug, Cristal, Salon, Comtes de Champagne, etc. — **90–98+**.

### Sauternes

- **First growth Sauternes:** d'Yquem; **classed growths** (Rieussec, Climens, etc.) — **90–98+** in top vintages.

## Representative names only

The lists above are **representative**, not exhaustive. Many other estates in these regions also score 90+ in strong vintages. For a **specific château or domaine and vintage**, always use **WEB_SEARCH** or direct the user to Wine Advocate, Vinous, or Decanter for the exact score.
