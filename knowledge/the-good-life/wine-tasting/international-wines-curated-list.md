## Afrique du Sud — South Africa (Stellenbosch)

- **Klein Constantia — Vin de Constance — 500 ml 2008** — **Sweet** Constantia-style wine (Muscat de Frontignan); historic style, iconic. Honey, apricot, orange blossom; 500 ml bottle. Serve 8–10 °C; dessert, blue cheese, foie gras. Age-worthy. See south-african-wines.md.
- **Anwilka 2005** — Stellenbosch **red** (Bordeaux blend; Klein Constantia + Bruno Prats partnership). Structured, age-worthy. 2005 drinking now or hold. Decant 30–45 min; 16–18 °C; lamb, steak.

## Chili — Chile

- **Casa Lapostolle — La Casa — Blanc 2011** — Lapostolle (Colchagua / Apalta); **white** La Casa: Chardonnay or blend; rich, New World. 10–12 °C; poultry, seafood.
- **Casa Lapostolle — Alexandre Syrah 2010** — **Red** Syrah from Lapostolle’s top range (Apalta). 2010 drinking now; decant 30 min; 16–18 °C; lamb, grilled meat.

## Espagne — Spain

- **Dominio de Pingus — PSI — Ribera del Duero 2011** — Peter Sisseck project; **PSI** = accessible Ribera del Duero (Tempranillo). 2011 drinking now; decant 30–45 min; 16–18 °C.
- **Dominio de Pingus — Flor de Pingus — Ribera del Duero 2011** — **Flor de Pingus** = mid-tier Pingus; Ribera del Duero. Rich, structured. Decant 45 min; 16–18 °C.
- **Dominio de Pingus — Pingus — Ribera del Duero 2011** — **Pingus** = flagship; one of Spain’s most sought-after reds. 2011 in a good window; decant 1 hour; 16–18 °C; landmark wine.
- **Gloria de Ostatu — Rioja Alavesa 2004** — Rioja (Alavesa); Ostatu estate. 2004 mature; red fruit, spice, oak. 16–18 °C; decant briefly. Same for 2003, 2002 below.
- **Gloria de Ostatu — Rioja Alavesa 2003**
- **Gloria de Ostatu — Rioja Alavesa 2002**
- **Clos Erasmus — Priorat 1995** — Daphne Glorian; **Priorat** (Garnacha, Syrah, etc.). 1995 mature; powerful, mineral. Decant briefly; 16–18 °C; lamb, game.

## Italie — Italy

- **Solaia — Toscana IGT 2011** — Antinori; **Cabernet Sauvignon / Sangiovese** blend. 2011 in a good window; decant 45–60 min; 16–18 °C; steak, lamb.
- **Masseto — Toscana IGT 2011** — **100% Merlot** (Tenuta dell’Ornellaia, Bolgheri); one of Italy’s most iconic reds. 2011 structured; decant 45–60 min; 16–18 °C; landmark wine.
- **Barolo — Le Vigne — Sandrone 2005** — Luciano Sandrone **Barolo Le Vigne** (blend of vineyards). 2005 Nebbiolo; tar, rose, red fruit; decant 45–60 min; 16–18 °C; truffle, game, aged cheese.

## Allemagne — Germany

- **Schloss Schönborn — Rheingau — Riesling Spätlese — Blanc 1994** — **Rheingau** Riesling Spätlese; 1994 mature; honey, petrol, lime. 8–10 °C; dessert or aged character with cheese.

## When to use this list

- **South Africa:** Klein Constantia Vin de Constance (sweet), Anwilka (red); see also south-african-wines.md for full SA coverage.
- **Chile:** Casa Lapostolle La Casa blanc, Alexandre Syrah.
- **Spain:** Pingus/PSI/Flor de Pingus (Ribera del Duero), Gloria de Ostatu (Rioja), Clos Erasmus (Priorat).
- **USA Napa:** Dominus (any vintage), Joseph Phelps Insignia.
- **Italy:** Solaia, Masseto (Tuscany), Sandrone Barolo Le Vigne.
- **Germany:** Diel Goldloch Spätlese, Schloss Schönborn Rheingau Spätlese (sweet/off-dry Riesling).
- **Thailand:** Domaine Granmonte Chenin blanc, The Orient Syrah.
- **Canada:** Pentâge and Gehringer Brothers ice wines (dessert).

For exact drinking window or score for a specific bottle, use WEB_SEARCH if needed.

## Service (summary)

- **Dry whites:** 8–10 °C (Chenin, La Casa). **Sweet / Spätlese / ice wine:** 8–10 °C; small pours with dessert or cheese.
- **Reds:** 16–18 °C; decant Napa, Pingus, Solaia, Masseto, Barolo 45–60 min; older bottles briefly or not at all.
