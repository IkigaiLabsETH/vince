# Pauillac, Médoc, Bordeaux — Beyond Expert

## Overview

**Pauillac** is the heart of the Left Bank: **Cabernet Sauvignon–dominant**, gravel soils, **three of the five 1855 first growths** (Château Lafite Rothschild, Château Latour, Château Mouton Rothschild). Wines are **structured, cassis-driven, tannic in youth**, built for long aging. Pauillac defines "classic" Médoc for many drinkers.

## Terroir

- **Graves (gravel)** from the Gironde; excellent drainage; Cabernet Sauvignon excels. Subtle variations between northern Pauillac (near Saint-Estèphe) and southern (near Saint-Julien) and between châteaux; **Latour** has riverside gravel; **Lafite** and **Mouton** on plateau and slopes.

## 1855 Classification in Pauillac

### First growths (1er cru)

- **Château Lafite Rothschild:** **Elegance, perfume, finesse;** long-lived. 95–100 in top vintages.
- **Château Latour:** **Power, density, consistency;** among the most age-worthy. 95–100.
- **Château Mouton Rothschild:** **Rich, opulent, artistic;** promoted to first growth in 1973. 95–100.

### Other key châteaux (often 90+)

- **Château Pichon Longueville Comtesse de Lalande (Pichon Comtesse):** **Elegant, aromatic;** often more Merlot; 90–95+.
- **Château Pichon Longueville Baron (Pichon Baron):** **Structured, classic;** 90–95+.
- **Château Lynch-Bages:** **Reliable, generous;** "people's first growth"; 90–95+.
- **Château Pontet-Canet:** **Biodynamic, precise;** 90–95+.
- **Château Grand-Puy-Lacoste:** **Classic, value;** 90+.
- **Château Haut-Batailley, Duhart-Milon (Milon AOC but same family as Lafite), Clerc Milon, etc.:** All serious Pauillac/Milon names.

## Style and profile

- **Classic Pauillac:** Cassis, blackcurrant, cedar, pencil lead, violet; full body; firm tannins; 10–30+ years aging. **Decant** young wines 1–2 hours.
- **Vs Saint-Julien:** Pauillac often **firmer and more powerful**; Saint-Julien often more **refined and balanced**. Vs **Saint-Estèphe:** Pauillac more **refined**; Saint-Estèphe **firmer, more rustic** tannins.

## Vintage (short)

2015, 2016, 2018, 2019, 2020 strong. For "drink now" suggest 2014 or 2016 (if ready); for cellaring 2019, 2020. First growths and top seconds: 15–40+ years.

## Service

- **Temperature:** 16–18 °C. **Decant** young (under 12 years) 60–90 min; older 30–60 min, careful with sediment.
