# French Wine Producers — Quick Reference

One-line reference per producer so Kelly can name names when asked "who makes the best X" or "which producer for Y." Use with region files for full context.

## Bordeaux

- **Château Margaux, Lafite Rothschild, Latour, Mouton Rothschild, Haut-Brion:** First growths; reference Left Bank (and Haut-Brion Graves). Cellar 15–30+ years.
- **Cheval Blanc, Ausone, Figeac:** Saint-Émilion; Merlot/Cabernet Franc; top tier.
- **Petrus, Le Pin, Lafleur, Vieux Château Certan:** Pomerol; Merlot-led; among the most sought-after.
- **Palmer, Léoville Las Cases, Ducru-Beaucaillou, Pichon Comtesse:** Médoc; classic, age-worthy.
- **Château Olivier, Domaine de Chevalier, Smith Haut Lafitte:** Pessac-Léognan; red and white; classed growths.
- **Château d'Yquem, Climens, Rieussec, Suduiraut, Guiraud:** Sauternes/Barsac; sweet; Yquem premier cru supérieur.

## Burgundy

- **Raveneau, Dauvissat, William Fèvre:** Chablis; mineral, precise.
- **Comtes Lafon, Roulot, Coche-Dury, Arnaud Ente:** Meursault; rich white Burgundy.
- **Leflaive, Henri Boillot:** Puligny-Montrachet / Chassagne; tension, mineral.
- **Domaine de la Romanée-Conti (DRC), Leroy, Méo-Camuzet:** Vosne-Romanée; top red and white.
- **Armand Rousseau, Dujac, Bruno Clair:** Gevrey-Chambertin; structured red.
- **Comte de Vogüé, Barthod:** Chambolle-Musigny; fragrant red.
- **Lafarge, d'Angerville:** Volnay; delicate red.
- **Louis Jadot, Faiveley, Bouchard Père:** Négociants; broad range.

## Rhône

- **Guigal:** Northern Rhône; Hermitage, Côte-Rôtie (La Mouline, La Landonne, La Turque), Crozes-Hermitage; reference producer.
- **Chapoutier, Jean-Louis Chave:** Hermitage; biodynamic (Chapoutier), traditional (Chave).
- **Delas, Yves Cuilleron:** Saint-Joseph, Condrieu; Cuilleron top for Condrieu.
- **Château de Beaucastel, Clos des Papes, Vieux Télégraphe, Rayas, Pegau:** Châteauneuf-du-Pape; reference Southern Rhône.

## Loire

- **Pascal Cotat, François Cotat, Didier Dagueneau:** Sancerre / Pouilly-Fumé; mineral, precise.
- **Domaine Huet, François Chidaine:** Vouvray; sec to moelleux; age-worthy Chenin.
- **Domaine de la Pépière, Luneau-Papin:** Muscadet sur lie; saline, oysters.
- **Bernard Baudry, Olga Raffault, Charles Joguet:** Chinon; Cabernet Franc.
- **Gratien & Meyer, Langlois-Château:** Crémant de Loire; value bubbles.

## Champagne

- **Dom Pérignon, Krug, Louis Roederer (Cristal), Taittinger (Comtes), Pol Roger (Churchill):** Prestige cuvées; celebration, impress.
- **Ruinart, Taittinger, Pol Roger, Bollinger:** Houses; NV and vintage; reliable.
- **Egly-Ouriet, Pierre Péters, Jacques Selosse, Larmandier-Bernier, Agrapart:** Grower Champagne; terroir-driven.

## Alsace

- **Trimbach:** Riesling (Clos Sainte Hune), Gewurztraminer, Pinot Gris; dry, classic.
- **Zind-Humbrecht, Domaine Weinbach, Hugel:** Riesling, Gewurz, Pinot Gris; aromatic.
- **Marcel Deiss:** Blend-focused; field blends, grand cru.

## Beaujolais

- **Jean Foillard, Marcel Lapierre, Jean-Paul Thévenet, Guy Breton:** Morgon "Gang of Four"; serious cru Beaujolais.
- **Jules Desjourneys:** Fleurie; top tier.
- **Château des Jacques (Louis Jadot), Jean-Paul Brun (Terres Dorées):** Moulin-à-Vent, Brouilly; structured and value.
