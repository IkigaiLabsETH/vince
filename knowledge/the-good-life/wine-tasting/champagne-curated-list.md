## Brut non millésimés (Non-vintage Brut)

- **Selosse — Substance** — Jacques Selosse; **solera-style** perpetual reserve; oxidative, complex, cult grower Champagne. 6–8 °C; apéritif, contemplation.
- **Selosse — Le Mesnil les Carelles** — Selosse single-site (Le Mesnil-sur-Oger); blanc de blancs, precise, mineral. 6–8 °C.
- **Krug — Grande Cuvée** — Krug’s multi-vintage blend; rich, brioche, depth. Reference NV. 6–8 °C; celebration, oysters.
- **Selosse — Version Originale (VO)** — Selosse NV; oxidative, distinctive. 6–8 °C.
- **Ruinart — Le « R » de Ruinart** — Ruinart’s prestige NV (Chardonnay-led). 6–8 °C; refined apéritif.
- **Egly-Ouriet — Grand Cru Tradition** — Grower; Ambonnay/Pinot-led or blend; full, vinous. 6–8 °C.
- **Laurent Perrier** — House NV brut; clean, consistent. 6–8 °C.
- **Bollinger — Spéciale Cuvée** — Bollinger NV; Pinot-led, vinous, robust. 6–8 °C; oysters, canapés.
- **Roederer — Brut Premier** — Louis Roederer NV; balanced, fine. 6–8 °C.
- **Billecart-Salmon — Brut Réserve** — House NV; elegant, fresh. 6–8 °C.
- **Henry Giraud — François Hémart** — Aÿ; often oak-influenced, rich. 6–8 °C.
- **Thienot** — House NV; reliable. 6–8 °C.
- **Martel — Victoire Prestige** — House NV. 6–8 °C.
- **Clos du Château de Bligny** — Rare; check if NV or vintage. 6–8 °C.

## Millésimés (Vintage)

- **Larmandier-Bernier — Terre de Vertus 2008** — Grower; Vertus (Côte des Blancs); 2008 excellent vintage. Blanc de blancs, mineral. 6–8 °C; oysters, fine apéritif.
- **Taittinger — Comtes de Champagne 2005** — Prestige blanc de blancs; 2005 in a good window. 6–8 °C; landmark.
- **Bollinger — Grande Année 2004** — Bollinger vintage; 2004 drinking well. 6–8 °C; decant optional for older bottles.
- **Dom Pérignon 2004** — Moët prestige; 2004 in a good window. 6–8 °C; celebration.
- **Selosse 2003** — Jacques Selosse vintage; oxidative, complex. 6–8 °C.
- **Selosse 2002** — Same; 2002 strong vintage. 6–8 °C.
- **Louis Roederer — Cristal 2002** — **Cristal**; 2002 excellent. 6–8 °C; landmark.
- **Egly-Ouriet — Grand Cru Brut 2002** — Grower vintage; Ambonnay. 6–8 °C.
- **Krug — Clos du Mesnil 2000** — **Single-vineyard** blanc de blancs (Le Mesnil); Krug’s Chardonnay icon. 2000 in a good window. 6–8 °C; landmark.
- **Salon — Magnum 1999** — Salon (Le Mesnil only); **magnum** ages slowly. 1999 in a good window. 6–8 °C; landmark.
- **Krug — Clos du Mesnil 1988** — 1988 mature; honey, brioche, developed. 6–8 °C; handle with care.
- **Krug — Clos d’Ambonnay 1998** — **Blanc de noirs** from Ambonnay (Pinot); rare, powerful. 6–8 °C; landmark.
- **Dom Pérignon 1982** — 1982 mature; rich, developed. 6–8 °C; decant briefly optional.
- **Dom Pérignon 1964** — Very old; handle with care; brief decant or none. 6–8 °C; landmark occasion.

## When to use this list

- **Brut NV / apéritif / value:** Lead with Krug Grande Cuvée, Bollinger Spéciale Cuvée, Ruinart R, Billecart-Salmon Brut Réserve, or grower (Selosse, Egly-Ouriet).
- **Rosé:** Krug Rosé, Ruinart R rosé, Billecart-Salmon Brut Rosé, Laurent Perrier Rosé, Bollinger Rosé.
- **Vintage / prestige / landmark:** Dom Pérignon (2004, 1982, 1964), Cristal 2002, Krug Clos du Mesnil (2000, 1988), Krug Clos d’Ambonnay 1998, Salon 1999 magnum, Taittinger Comtes 2005, Selosse 2002/2003, Larmandier-Bernier Terre de Vertus 2008.
- **Specific bottle from this list:** Use the entry above; for exact drinking window, use WEB_SEARCH if needed.

## Service (summary)

- **Temperature:** 6–8 °C. Do not overchill; 15–20 min in ice bucket is enough.
- **Glass:** Flute or tulip (tulip preferred for aroma). Open without pop; twist base, hold cork.
- **Old vintage (e.g. 1964, 1982, 1988):** Decant briefly or not at all; handle gently.
