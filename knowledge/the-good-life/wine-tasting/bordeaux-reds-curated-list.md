# Les vins rouges de Bordeaux — Curated List (Grands Vins)

Curated list of **Bordeaux red wines** (grands vins by appellation) for Kelly. Use when the user asks for a specific château, appellation, or bottle from this list. See also **bordeaux-overview.md**, **bordeaux-seconds-vins.md**, **margaux.md**, **pessac-leognan.md**, **saint-emilion-pomerol.md**.

**Service (all reds):** 16–18 °C. Decant young Left Bank (Pauillac, Saint-Estèphe, Saint-Julien, Margaux) 45–90 min; Right Bank (Saint-Émilion, Pomerol) 30–45 min; older bottles (15+ years) briefly or 30 min. **Pairing:** Lamb, steak, daube, game, cheese.

---

## Les Premiers de Bordeaux (First Growths & Premiers Grands Crus Classés A)

**1855 First Growths (Médoc + Haut-Brion):** Lafite, Latour, Margaux, Mouton Rothschild (Pauillac/Margaux), Haut-Brion (Pessac-Léognan). **Saint-Émilion Premier Grand Cru Classé A (2012):** Angélus, Ausone, Cheval Blanc, Pavie. **Pomerol (no classification):** Pétrus is the reference. Use this section when the user asks for "first growth," "premier cru," "best Bordeaux," or a specific bottle below.

### Saint-Émilion (Premiers Grands Crus Classés A)

- **Château Angélus** 2004, 2003, 2001, 2000, 1989 · **Château Angélus — magnum 1955** (landmark; decant briefly). Merlot-led, opulent; 30–45 min decant for younger vintages.
- **Château Ausone 1999** — Ausone (A); Cabernet Franc–led, limestone; 1999 in a good window. Decant 30–45 min.
- **Château Cheval Blanc 1989** — Cheval Blanc (A); Cabernet Franc–led, silky. 1989 mature; decant briefly or 30 min; landmark.
- **Château Pavie** 2003, 2002, 1998 — Pavie (A); powerful, modern. Decant 45–60 min for 2002/2003; 1998 in a good window.

### Pomerol

- **Pétrus 1997** — Reference Pomerol; Merlot-dominant. 1997 drinking now; decant 30–45 min; landmark.

### Pessac-Léognan

- **Château Haut-Brion 1996** — First growth (1855); 1996 in a good window; refined, complex. Decant 45–60 min; landmark.

### Pauillac (1855 First Growths)

- **Château Lafite Rothschild 2000** — First growth; 2000 landmark vintage. Decant 1 hour; landmark.
- **Château Latour** 1999, **1962** — First growth; 1999 drinking now; 1962 handle with care, decant briefly only.
- **Château Mouton Rothschild** 1990, 1989, 1997, **1970** — First growth; 1990/1989 in a good window; 1970 mature, decant briefly; landmark.

### Margaux

- **Château Margaux 1997** — First growth; 1997 drinking now; elegant, perfumed. Decant 30–45 min.
