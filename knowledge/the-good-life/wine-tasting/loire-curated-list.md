## Les vins blancs secs de la Loire (Dry White Wines of the Loire)

### Pouilly-Fumé (Sauvignon Blanc)

- **Domaine Jolivet — « Terre Blanche » 2013** — Pouilly-Fumé from terres blanches (clay-limestone); richer, rounder. 8–10 °C; goat cheese, shellfish.
- **Domaine Jolivet — « Indigène » 2012** — Pouilly-Fumé cuvée; Jolivet is a serious producer. 8–10 °C.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Blanc Fumé de Pouilly 2012** — Dagueneau’s entry-level Pouilly-Fumé; already precise and mineral. 8–10 °C.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Blanc Fumé de Pouilly 2011** — Same cuvée; 2011 drinking now.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Buisson Renard 2012** — Single-vineyard Pouilly-Fumé; flinty, tension, long. 8–10 °C; oysters, goat cheese.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Buisson Renard 2011** — Same; 2011 in a good window.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Pur Sang 2012** — Top Dagueneau cuvée; powerful, mineral, age-worthy. 90–95+ in top vintages. 8–10 °C; do not overchill.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Pur Sang 2011** — Same; 2011 drinking now or short hold.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Silex 2012** — Silex (flint) soils; gunflint, smoke, linear; legendary cuvée. 8–10 °C.
- **Domaine Didier Dagueneau — Silex 2011** — Same; 2011 in a good window.

### Sancerre (Sauvignon Blanc)

- **Domaine Jolivet — Le Clos du Roy 2013** — Sancerre; Le Clos du Roy is a notable parcel. 8–10 °C; Crottin de Chavignol, oysters.
- **Domaine Alphonse Mellot — La Moussière 2013** — Sancerre La Moussière (silex); Mellot is a reference. Flinty, precise. 8–10 °C.
- **Domaine Pascal Cotat — Les Monts Damnés 2012** — Sancerre from Chavignol’s steep Monts Damnés; terres blanches, rich, age-worthy. 8–10 °C; goat cheese, shellfish.
- **Edmond Vatan — Clos La Néore 2013** — Sancerre Clos La Néore (Chavignol); rare, legendary, terres blanches. 8–10 °C; landmark Sancerre.
- **Domaine Alphonse Mellot — Edmond 2011** — Sancerre cuvée Edmond; top tier from Mellot. 8–10 °C.
- **Domaine Alphonse Mellot — Les Romains 2012** — Sancerre Les Romains; silex, mineral. 8–10 °C.

### Saumur Blanc (Chenin Blanc)

- **Château de Fosse-Sèche — Arcane 2013** — Saumur blanc; Chenin, dry, mineral. 10–12 °C; fish, poultry.
- **Clos Rougeard — Brézé 2010** — Saumur Brézé (Chenin); Clos Rougeard is legendary. Dry, intense, age-worthy. 10–12 °C; serious white.
- **Thierry Germain — Insolite 2012** — Domaine des Roches Neuves; Saumur blanc Insolite; biodynamic, mineral. 10–12 °C.

### Vouvray (Chenin Blanc — sec = dry)

- **Domaine Huet — Clos du Bourg sec 2012** — Vouvray sec from Clos du Bourg; Huet is the reference for Vouvray. Dry, apple, honey, mineral; age-worthy. 10–12 °C; fish, poultry, goat cheese.
- **Domaine Gilles Gaudron 2003** — Vouvray (sec or demi-sec depending on cuvée); 2003 hot vintage; drink now. 10–12 °C.

## Les vins rouges de la Loire (Red Wines of the Loire)

### Chinon (Cabernet Franc)

- **Bernard Baudry — « Le Domaine » 2011** — Chinon; Baudry is a reference. Le Domaine: classic, red fruit, pencil lead. 12–14 °C; lamb, duck, chill lightly for lunch.
- **Bernard Baudry — « Les Grézeaux » 2011** — Chinon parcel Les Grézeaux; more structure, gravel soils. 12–14 °C; decant 15–30 min if young.
- **Bernard Baudry — « Le Clos Guillot » 2011** — Chinon Le Clos Guillot; limestone, fine tannin. 12–14 °C.
- **Philippe Alliet — « Vieilles Vignes » 2012** — Chinon vieilles vignes; Alliet is a top Chinon producer. 12–14 °C; lamb, grilled meat.
- **Philippe Alliet — « L’Huisserie » 2012** — Chinon L’Huisserie; parcel or cuvée. 12–14 °C.

### Saumur-Champigny (Cabernet Franc)

- **Thierry Germain — Domaine des Roches Neuves — « Terres Chaudes » 2012** — Saumur-Champigny; biodynamic, Terres Chaudes parcel. Red fruit, herbs, fine. 12–14 °C; lamb, duck.
- **Thierry Germain — « La Marginale » 2011** — Saumur-Champigny La Marginale; top cuvée from Roches Neuves. 12–14 °C; decant 15–30 min.
- **Clos Rougeard — « Le Clos » 2010** — Saumur-Champigny Le Clos; Clos Rougeard is one of the Loire’s greatest estates. 2010 structured, age-worthy. Decant 30 min; 12–14 °C; landmark red.
- **Clos Rougeard — « Les Poyeux » 2010** — Saumur-Champigny Les Poyeux; another top Rougeard cuvée. 2010 in a good window. Decant 30 min; 12–14 °C.

## When to use this list

- **Loire white / Pouilly-Fumé / Sancerre / Saumur blanc / Vouvray sec:** Use the "Vins blancs secs" section; lead with Dagueneau (Silex, Pur Sang, Buisson Renard), Cotat Monts Damnés, Vatan Clos La Néore, Mellot, Huet Clos du Bourg sec, Clos Rougeard Brézé, or Jolivet.
- **Loire red / Chinon / Saumur-Champigny:** Use the "Vins rouges" section; lead with Baudry, Alliet, Clos Rougeard Le Clos/Les Poyeux, or Thierry Germain Terres Chaudes/La Marginale.
- **Specific bottle from this list:** Use the entry above; for exact drinking window or score, use WEB_SEARCH if needed.

## Service (summary)

- **Whites (Sancerre, Pouilly-Fumé, Saumur blanc, Vouvray sec):** 8–10 °C for Sauvignon; 10–12 °C for Chenin (Saumur, Vouvray). Do not overchill.
- **Reds (Chinon, Saumur-Champigny):** 12–14 °C; can serve lightly chilled in summer. Decant structured Clos Rougeard or top Baudry/Alliet 15–30 min.
