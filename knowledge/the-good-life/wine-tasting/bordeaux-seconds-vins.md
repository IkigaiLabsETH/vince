# Bordeaux Seconds Vins — The Complete Guide

Second wines are made from younger vines, declassified parcels, or lots that don't make the grand vin. They offer the DNA of a great château at 30-50% of the price, often drinking well 5-10 years earlier. For the connoisseur: seconds vins are where Bordeaux value lives.

## How Seconds Vins Work
- **Same terroir, same team.** The winemaker, the chai, the philosophy — identical. The fruit just comes from different parcels or vine ages.
- **Drink earlier.** Less extraction, less new oak, more approachable young. Most drink well at 5-15 years vs 15-30+ for the grand vin.
- **Price:** Typically 25-40% of the grand vin. A €50-80 second wine from a first growth is tasting Lafite/Latour/Margaux DNA.

## KELLY'S SECONDS VINS PLAYBOOK

### The "I Want First Growth Taste Under €130" Picks
1. **Pavillon Rouge du Château Margaux** — €80-130. The king of seconds.
2. **Les Forts de Latour** — €80-130. Sometimes approaches Latour in quality.
3. **Carruades de Lafite** — €80-120. Lafite elegance, more Merlot softness.
4. **Le Petit Cheval** — €80-120. Cheval Blanc DNA, silky Cab Franc.
5. **Le Clarence de Haut-Brion** — €80-120. Renamed, underpriced for what it is.

### The "Best Under €50" Picks
1. **La Croix de Beaucaillou** (Ducru) — €30-50. Refined Saint-Julien.
2. **Pagodes de Cos** — €30-50. Mini-Cos d'Estournel.
3. **Alter Ego de Palmer** — €40-65. Palmer perfume.
4. **Réserve de la Comtesse** — €35-55. Pichon Comtesse finesse.
5. **No. 3 d'Angélus** — €30-50. Right Bank generosity.

### The "Best Under €25" Picks (Weeknight Bordeaux)
1. **Les Fiefs de Lagrange** — €15-22. Best value in Saint-Julien.
2. **Écho de Lynch-Bages** — €25-40. Classic Pauillac.
3. **Lacoste-Borie** — €20-30. Grand-Puy-Lacoste's gem.
4. **Baron de Brane** — €18-28. Easy-drinking Margaux.
5. **Sarget de Gruaud Larose** — €18-28. Generous Saint-Julien.

### Service
- **All seconds:** 16-18°C. Decant 30-45 minutes (less than grand vin — they're built to be more approachable).
- **Young (2019-2022):** Decant 45 min. Ready to drink but will improve 5-10 years.
- **Best recent vintages for seconds:** 2015, 2016, 2018, 2019, 2020 — all excellent across Bordeaux.
- **The move:** Buy 2016 seconds now. They're in their first drinking window and the vintage is historic.
