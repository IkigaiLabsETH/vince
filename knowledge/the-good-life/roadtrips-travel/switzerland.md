## Practical Information

### Driving
- **Vignette**: Annual highway sticker required (CHF 40)
- **Speed Limits**: 120 km/h highways, 80 km/h rural, 50 km/h urban
- **Mountain Passes**: Some close winter, check conditions
- **Tunnels**: Gotthard can have queues

### Best Seasons
- **Summer (June-September)**: All passes open, hiking
- **Fall (September-October)**: Colors, fewer crowds
- **Winter**: Ski resorts, some roads closed
- **Spring (April-May)**: Variable, snow at altitude

### Currency
- Swiss Franc (CHF)
- Expensive country, budget accordingly
