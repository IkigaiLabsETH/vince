## Practical Information

### Driving
- **Toll Roads**: Via Verde electronic or easy toll
- **Speed Limits**: 120 km/h highways, 90 rural, 50 urban
- **Parking**: Generally easier than Spain
- **Roads**: Excellent quality, some narrow rural

### Best Seasons
- **Spring (April-May)**: Wildflowers, pleasant
- **Fall (September-October)**: Harvest, warm
- **Summer**: Hot interior, crowded Algarve
- **Winter**: Mild Lisbon/Algarve, green north

### Value Proposition
- Significantly cheaper than France/Italy
- Wine excellent value
- Restaurants more affordable
- Hotels competitive
