# Roadtrip: Italy

## Overview

Italy offers endless roadtrip possibilities across diverse landscapes: Alpine Dolomites, Tuscan hills, Amalfi coast, and Sicilian countryside. Each region has distinct character, cuisine, and wine traditions.

## Key Regions

### Northern Italy
- **Lakes (Como, Garda, Maggiore)**: Villa-lined shores
- **Dolomites**: Dramatic Alpine driving
- **Piedmont**: Truffle country, Barolo wines
- **Veneto**: Venice region, Prosecco hills

### Central Italy
- **Tuscany**: Classic Italian roadtrip territory
- **Umbria**: "Green heart" of Italy, truffle country
- **Le Marche**: Undiscovered Tuscany alternative
- **Emilia-Romagna**: Motor Valley, gastronomy

### Southern Italy & Islands
- **Amalfi Coast**: Iconic coastal drive
- **Puglia**: Heel of boot, trulli houses
- **Sicily**: Ancient sites, Mt. Etna, baroque cities
- **Sardinia**: Costa Smeralda glamour, wild interior

## Practical Information

### Driving
- **ZTL Zones**: Historic center restricted areas, check carefully
- **Autostrada**: Toll highways, Telepass useful
- **Speed Limits**: 130 km/h highways, 110 rural, 50 urban
- **Parking**: Blue lines = paid, white = free

### Best Seasons
- **Spring (April-May)**: Perfect weather, fewer crowds
- **Fall (September-October)**: Harvest, truffles, colors
- **Summer**: Crowded coasts, hot cities
- **Winter**: Ski north, mild south

### Regional Considerations
- **Amalfi**: Best shoulder season, summer very crowded
- **Tuscany**: September/October ideal (vendemmia)
- **Dolomites**: Summer for driving, winter for skiing
- **Sicily**: Spring or fall, summer very hot
