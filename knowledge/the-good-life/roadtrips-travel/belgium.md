# Roadtrip: Belgium

## Overview

Belgium offers concentrated treasures: medieval cities, world-class gastronomy, legendary beer culture, and art from Van Eyck to contemporary. Small size makes it perfect for focused trips combining multiple cities and experiences.

## Key Regions

### Flanders (Dutch-speaking)
- **Bruges**: Medieval perfection, canals
- **Ghent**: University city, Van Eyck altarpiece
- **Antwerp**: Fashion, diamonds, Rubens
- **Leuven**: Beer culture, KU Leuven

### Brussels Capital
- **Grand Place**: UNESCO square
- **Art Nouveau**: Horta architecture
- **EU Quarter**: European institutions
- **Marolles**: Flea market, character

### Wallonia (French-speaking)
- **Liège**: Industrial heritage, nightlife
- **Namur**: Citadel, Meuse confluence
- **Dinant**: Saxophones, cliffs
- **Ardennes**: Forest, nature, chateaux

### Beer Regions
- **Pajottenland**: Lambic heartland
- **Trappist Routes**: Abbey breweries
- **Flemish Countryside**: Traditional brewing

## Classic Routes

### Art Cities Triangle
- **Route**: Brussels → Bruges → Ghent → Antwerp → Brussels
- **Distance**: ~300 km loop
- **Duration**: 5-7 days
- **Highlights**: Medieval art, museums, canals

### Beer Pilgrimage
- **Route**: Brussels → Pajottenland → Westmalle → Westvleteren
- **Distance**: ~200 km
- **Duration**: 3-4 days
- **Highlights**: Lambic producers, Trappist abbeys

### Wallonia Nature & Heritage
- **Route**: Brussels → Namur → Dinant → Ardennes → Liège
- **Distance**: ~250 km
- **Duration**: 4-5 days
- **Highlights**: Castles, caves, forests

### Flanders Fields (History)
- **Route**: Brussels → Ypres → Passchendaele → Bruges
- **Distance**: ~200 km
- **Duration**: 2-3 days
- **Highlights**: WWI memorials, In Flanders Fields museum

## Practical Information

### Driving
- **No Tolls**: Highway network free
- **Speed Limits**: 120 km/h highways, 70/90 rural, 50 urban
- **Parking**: Challenging in cities, use P+R
- **Brussels Ring**: Can be congested

### Best Seasons
- **Spring (April-May)**: Flowers, pleasant
- **Fall (September-October)**: Beer festivals, mild
- **Christmas**: Markets in every city
- **Summer**: Tourist peak, good terrace weather

### Compact Country
- Brussels to Bruges: 1 hour
- Brussels to Antwerp: 45 minutes
- Brussels to Ghent: 35 minutes
- Day trips very feasible

## Suggested Itineraries

### Art Cities Weekend (3-4 days)
- Day 1: Brussels, Grand Place, museums
- Day 2: Bruges, canals, Memling
- Day 3: Ghent, Van Eyck altarpiece
- Day 4: Antwerp, Rubens (optional)

### Gastronomy Focus (5 days)
- Day 1-2: Brussels, Bon Bon, Cantillon
- Day 3: Ghent, Chambre Séparée
- Day 4: Bruges, Hertog Jan
- Day 5: Return via Hof Van Cleve

### Beer Pilgrimage (4 days)
- Day 1: Brussels, Cantillon, Moeder Lambic
- Day 2: Pajottenland lambic tour
- Day 3: Trappist abbeys (Westmalle, Westvleteren)
- Day 4: Bruges beer bars

### Complete Belgium (7 days)
- Day 1-2: Brussels
- Day 3: Ghent
- Day 4-5: Bruges, Flanders
- Day 6: Antwerp
- Day 7: Return Brussels
