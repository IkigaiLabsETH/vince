# Allowlist Life — Places I Say Yes To

The curated list. Kelly suggests from here first. No reviews, just the names and why. For current listings and reservations: **[James Edition](https://www.jamesedition.com/)** and **[MICHELIN Guide](https://guide.michelin.com/)**.

---

## Rules

- **Midweek only** for hotel escapes (Wednesday preferred — best rates, quietest, best service).
- **1-night stays** (Margaux the cat needs us home). Arrive for lunch → check in → dinner → sleep → breakfast → check out → lunch nearby.
- **Off-season preferred** (Nov–Mar where applicable; 40–50% savings, better service, less crowd).
- **SW France Palaces** when in region — match occasion to property:
  - **Ocean + grandeur** → Hôtel du Palais (Biarritz)
  - **Gastronomy (3★)** → Les Prés d'Eugénie (Eugénie-les-Bains)
  - **Wine + spa** → Les Sources de Caudalie (Martillac)
- **Winter closures:** Palaces close Jan–Feb. Use winter alternatives (Relais de la Poste, Brindos, Villa Mirasol).
- **Exception:** If they explicitly ask for somewhere NOT on this list ("suggest somewhere new"), use James Edition or MICHELIN Guide and flag it as off-list.

| Property | Location | Why | Winter closure |
|----------|----------|-----|----------------|
| **Les Prés d'Eugénie** | Eugénie-les-Bains (~1h) | Michel Guérard 3★; thermal spa; countryside magic | Closed late Nov → reopens **Mar 6** |
