## Rules

- **Wed only** for hotel escapes (midweek rates, less busy).
- **Southwest France Palaces** when in region: match occasion to property (Ocean → Palais Biarritz; Gastronomy → Eugénie; Wine + spa → Caudalie).
- **Off-season preferred** (Nov–Mar where applicable; 40–50% savings, better service).
- **1-night stays only** (cat Margaux needs us home). Arrive 13:00 for lunch at hotel restaurant; late checkout by 12:00.
- **Winter closures:** Palaces close Jan–Feb; use alternatives (Relais de la Poste, Brindos).

## Hotels

### Palaces Southwest France

- **Les Prés d'Eugénie** — Ultimate gastronomy (Michel Guérard 3★); thermal spa; countryside. Winter closure until Mar.
- **Hôtel du Palais** — Ocean drama, Grande Plage Biarritz, Napoleon III grandeur. Winter closure until Feb.
- **Les Sources de Caudalie** — Wine + spa immersion; La Grand'Vigne 2★; vineyard setting.
