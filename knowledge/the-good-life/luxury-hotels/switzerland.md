# Switzerland — Best 5-Star & Palace-Level Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Switzerland 5-star and palace-level hotels combine Alpine and lake settings with exceptional service and dining: Gstaad, St. Moritz, Geneva, Zurich, Lucerne, and the lakes.

## 5-Star / Palace-Level Hotels

- **Gstaad Palace** — Gstaad; iconic, spa, GreenGo, mountain views.
- **Badrutt's Palace Hotel** — St. Moritz; legend, lake, skiing, dining.
- **Beau-Rivage Palace** — Lausanne; Lake Geneva, gastronomy, gardens.
- **The Dolder Grand** — Zurich; hill above city, spa, art, two Michelin stars.
- **Bürgenstock Resort** — Lake Lucerne; Alpine resort, spa, views.
- **Victoria-Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa** — Interlaken; classic, spa, Jungfrau view.
- **Grand Hotel Kronenhof** — Pontresina; Engadin, Belle Époque, spa.
- **Le Mirador Resort & Spa** — Lake Geneva (Veytaux); view of lake, Montreux.
- **Palace Luzern** — Lucerne; lakefront, classic.
- **Mandarin Oriental, Geneva** — Geneva; Rhône, central.
- **Four Seasons Hotel des Bergues Geneva** — Geneva; lake, historic.
- **Park Hotel Vitznau** — Lake Lucerne; waterfront, Focus (2*), wine.

## Live the life

- **Alps (Gstaad, St. Moritz):** Gstaad Palace, Badrutt's Palace; ski, spa, grandeur.
- **Lakes (Geneva, Lucerne, Zurich):** Beau-Rivage, Dolder Grand, Bürgenstock; views, dining.
