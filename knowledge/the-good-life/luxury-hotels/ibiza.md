# Ibiza — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Ibiza 5-star hotels blend beach clubs, sunset spots, and relaxed luxury: north coast cliffs, Santa Eulalia, and Playa d'en Bossa for the ultimate Balearic stay.

## 5-Star Hotels

- **Hacienda Na Xamena** — North coast; clifftop, cascading pools, views; iconic.
- **Six Senses Ibiza** — Portinatx (north); resort, spa, sustainability, beach.
- **Nobu Hotel Ibiza Bay** — Talamanca (near Ibiza Town); beach, Nobu, pool.
- **Ushuaïa Ibiza Beach Hotel** — Playa d'en Bossa; pool parties, beach club, scene.
- **Hard Rock Hotel Ibiza** — Playa d'en Bossa; music, pool, family and party.
- **7Pines Resort Ibiza** — San José; cliffs, sunset, concierge, quiet luxury.
- **ME Ibiza** — Santa Eulalia; design, rooftop, beach.
- **Ibiza Gran Hotel** — Marina; central, pool, spa; reliable 5-star.
- **Agroturismo Atzaro** — Santa Eulalia (inland); country estate, spa, pool; rustic-luxe.
- **Can Bordoy** — Ibiza Town; garden house, 24 rooms, intimate.

## Live the life

- **North / sunset:** Hacienda Na Xamena, 7Pines, Six Senses; views, calm.
- **Beach / party:** Nobu Ibiza Bay, Ushuaïa; beach clubs, music.
- **Town / marina:** Ibiza Gran Hotel, Can Bordoy; dining, nightlife access.
