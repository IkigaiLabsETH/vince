# Spain — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Spain 5-star hotels cover Madrid, Barcelona, San Sebastián, Marbella, and beyond: historic palaces, beach resorts, and top gastronomy (San Sebastián’s star density).

## Madrid

- **The Ritz Madrid** — Prado; historic, Belmond, garden, dining.
- **Four Seasons Hotel Madrid** — Canalejas; new, roof terrace, Dani (3*).
- **Mandarin Oriental Ritz, Madrid** — Prado; restored palace, Deessa (1*).
- **Rosewood Villa Magna** — Salamanca; elegant, central.
- **Hotel Único Madrid** — Salamanca; boutique, design, Michelin dining.

## Barcelona

- **Hotel Arts Barcelona** — Port Olímpic; tower, sea, Enoteca (2*).
- **Mandarin Oriental, Barcelona** — Passeig de Gràcia; central, Moments (2*).
- **El Palace Barcelona** — Eixample; historic, rooftop, classic.
- **Majestic Hotel & Spa Barcelona** — Passeig de Gràcia; rooftop pool, dining.
- **Cotton House Hotel** — Eixample; heritage building, boutique.

## San Sebastián

- **Hotel María Cristina** — Centre; Belle Époque, river, Arzak and others nearby.
- **Akelarre** — Monte Igueldo; hotel and 3* restaurant, views.
- **NH Collection San Sebastián** — Central; reliable 5-star base for pintxos and stars.

## Marbella / Costa del Sol

- **Marbella Club Hotel** — Golden Mile; gardens, beach, classic.
- **Puente Romano Beach Resort** — Marbella; village feel, Nobu, sea.
- **Finca Cortesin** — Casares; golf, spa, Kabuki (1*).

## Other

- **Gran Hotel La Florida** — Barcelona (Tibidabo); hilltop, views, pool.
- **Parador de Santiago** — Santiago de Compostela; historic, pilgrimage.

## Live the life

- **Madrid:** Ritz, Four Seasons; art, dining, culture.
- **Barcelona:** Arts, Mandarin Oriental; sea, Gaudí, dining.
- **San Sebastián:** María Cristina; gastronomy, beach, pintxos.
- **Marbella:** Marbella Club, Puente Romano; sun, beach, golf.
