# New York — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

New York 5-star hotels define Manhattan (and Brooklyn) luxury: Central Park, Midtown theatres, Downtown finance, SoHo design, and world-class dining for the ultimate stay.

## 5-Star Hotels

- **The Plaza** — Fifth Avenue at Central Park South; historic icon, Palm Court, Oak Room.
- **Four Seasons Hotel New York** — Midtown (Downtown: 57 East 57th); tower, spa, dining; two locations.
- **Mandarin Oriental, New York** — Columbus Circle; park and Hudson views, spa, Asiate.
- **The Ritz-Carlton New York, Central Park** — Central Park South; park views, classic.
- **St. Regis New York** — Fifth Avenue; King Cole Bar, Butler service, Beaux Arts.
- **Baccarat Hotel New York** — Midtown; crystal, bar, refined.
- **The Mark** — Upper East Side; Renzo Piano, Jean-Georges, neighbourhood luxury.
- **Aman New York** — Crown Building, Fifth Avenue; Aman spa, Nama (Japanese); ultra-luxury.
- **Crosby Street Hotel** — SoHo; Firmdale, design, rooftop; boutique.
- **Equinox Hotel Hudson Yards** — Hudson Yards; fitness-led, views, rooftop.
- **The Beekman, A Thompson Hotel** — Financial District; historic atrium, Temple Court dining.
- **The Whitby Hotel** — Midtown; Firmdale, design, Orangery.
- **The Carlyle, A Rosewood Hotel** — Upper East Side; Bemelmans Bar, Café Carlyle, residential.
- **Park Lane New York** — Central Park South; new tower, views, rooftop.

## Live the life

- **Midtown / Central Park:** The Plaza, Ritz-Carlton, Mandarin Oriental, St. Regis; theatre, museums, park.
- **Downtown / Tribeca:** Crosby Street, Beekman; finance, dining, design.
- **Upper East Side / residential:** The Mark, Carlyle; neighbourhood, bars, culture.
- **Hudson Yards:** Equinox Hotel; modern, views, wellness.
