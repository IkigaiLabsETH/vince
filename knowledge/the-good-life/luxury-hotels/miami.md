# Miami — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Miami 5-star hotels span South Beach, Brickell, Key Biscayne, and Sunny Isles: oceanfront pools, Art Deco, urban towers, and wellness-focused resorts for the ultimate stay.

## 5-Star Hotels

- **Faena Hotel Miami Beach** — Miami Beach; Alan Faena, theatre, pool, dining; bold design.
- **Four Seasons Hotel Miami** — Brickell; urban, pool deck, bay views; business and leisure.
- **Mandarin Oriental, Miami** — Brickell; bay island, spa, La Mar by Gastón Acurio.
- **The Miami Beach EDITION** — South Beach; Ian Schrager, pool, nightlife, Basement.
- **The Setai, Miami Beach** — South Beach; Asian-inspired, three pools, quiet luxury.
- **1 Hotel South Beach** — South Beach; eco-luxury, rooftop, beach; design-led.
- **Carillon Miami Wellness Resort** — Miami Beach (north); spa focus, oceanfront, wellness.
- **W South Beach** — South Beach; beachfront, Living Room bar, scene.
- **Acqualina Resort & Residences** — Sunny Isles Beach; ocean, spa, family-friendly; north of Miami Beach.
- **Ritz-Carlton Key Biscayne** — Key Biscayne; island, pool, spa, quieter escape.
- **EAST, Miami** — Brickell; urban, rooftop pool, downtown.
- **The Goodtime Hotel** — South Beach; Pharrell Williams, pool, retro vibe.

## Live the life

- **South Beach:** Faena, EDITION, Setai, 1 Hotel; beach, pool scene, nightlife.
- **Brickell:** Four Seasons, Mandarin Oriental, EAST; urban, bay, business.
- **Key Biscayne / Sunny Isles:** Ritz-Carlton Key Biscayne, Acqualina; resort, quieter, family.
