# Lisbon — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Lisbon and surrounds offer 5-star stays from historic palaces to design-led towers: fado, tiles, river views, and easy access to Sintra and Cascais.

## 5-Star Hotels

- **Four Seasons Hotel Ritz Lisbon** — Central Lisbon; park views, rooftop gym, classic luxury.
- **Verride Palácio Santa Catarina** — Santa Catarina; 18th-century palace, river views, boutique.
- **Lumen Hotel & Wellness** — Príncipe Real; design, rooftop, wellness.
- **Palácio Belmonte** — Alfama; historic palace, 10 suites, gardens; intimate.
- **Olissippo Lapa Palace** — Lapa; palace on hill, pool, river; member Leading Hotels.
- **Pestana Palace Lisboa** — Belém; national monument, gardens, Villa Palácio.
- **Bairro Alto Hotel** — Bairro Alto; rooftop bar, central; design.
- **Sintra:** Tivoli Palácio de Seteais (palace, gardens); Lawrence's Hotel (historic).
- **Cascais:** Cascais Miragem; Grande Real Villa Itália; coastal 5-star.

## Live the life

- **Central Lisbon:** Ritz, Verride, Lapa Palace for river and history.
- **Sintra / Cascais:** Day trip or stay; palaces and coast.
