# Cape Town — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Cape Town offers world-class 5-star stays: Table Mountain and Atlantic views, V&A Waterfront, Camps Bay and Clifton beaches, wine country nearby. These are the top-tier hotels for the ultimate stay.

## 5-Star Hotels

- **One&Only Cape Town** — V&A Waterfront; island setting, Nobu, spa; urban waterfront luxury.
- **Ellerman House** — Bantry Bay; art collection, gardens, sea views; small, exclusive.
- **The Twelve Apostles Hotel and Spa** — Between Camps Bay and Llandudno; mountain and sea, spa, secluded.
- **Mount Nelson, A Belmond Hotel** — Gardens; pink palace, pool, afternoon tea; historic.
- **Cape Grace** — V&A Waterfront; yacht basin, library, personalized service.
- **The Silo** — V&A Waterfront; Zeitz MOCAA building, rooftop, design-led.
- **Queen Victoria Hotel** — Waterfront; boutique, Relais & Châteaux.
- **The Cellars-Hohenort** — Constantia; gardens, wine country, dining.

## Live the life

- **V&A Waterfront:** Dining, marina, Silo district; One&Only, Cape Grace, The Silo.
- **Camps Bay / Clifton:** Beach, Twelve Apostles; Atlantic sunsets.
- **Constantia:** Vineyards, peace; Cellars-Hohenort.
