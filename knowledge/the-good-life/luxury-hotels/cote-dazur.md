# Côte d'Azur — Best 5-Star & Palaces to Live the Life

## Overview

The Côte d'Azur (Nice to Saint-Tropez) has the highest concentration of French **palaces** and 5-star hotels: Cap-Ferrat, Antibes, Saint-Tropez, and legendary dining. For the **full list of palaces** here, see **france-palaces.md**.

## Palaces on the Côte d'Azur (see france-palaces.md)

- **Grand-Hôtel du Cap-Ferrat** — Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat; iconic, Club Dauphin, pool by the sea.
- **Hôtel du Cap-Eden-Roc** — Antibes; legendary, diving board, gardens.
- **Byblos Saint-Tropez** — Saint-Tropez; village vibe, pool, nightlife.
- **Château de la Messardière** — Saint-Tropez; hilltop, gardens.
- **Château de Saint-Martin & Spa** — Vence; Provençal estate, spa.
- **Cheval Blanc St-Tropez** — Saint-Tropez; LVMH, beach, La Vague d'Or (3*).
- **La Réserve Ramatuelle** — Ramatuelle; bay views, spa, villas.
- **La Bastide de Gordes** — Gordes (Provence); Luberon, village perch.

## Other 5-Star (non-palace)

- **La Réserve de Beaulieu** — Beaulieu-sur-Mer; Belle Époque, sea, Nautical Club.
- **Hotel Royal-Riviera** — Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat; gardens, pool, Mediterranean.
- **Château Eza** — Èze; perched village, views, intimate.
- **Hotel Negresco** — Nice; historic, Promenade des Anglais, art.

## Live the life

- **Cap-Ferrat / Antibes:** Eden-Roc, Grand-Hôtel du Cap-Ferrat; ultimate Riviera.
- **Saint-Tropez:** Byblos, Cheval Blanc, La Réserve Ramatuelle; beach and scene.
- **Beaulieu / Èze:** La Réserve de Beaulieu, Château Eza; quieter, views.
