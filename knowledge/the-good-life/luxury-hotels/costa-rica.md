# Costa Rica — Best 5-Star & Eco-Luxury Resorts to Live the Life

## Overview

Costa Rica 5-star and eco-luxury resorts combine beach, rainforest, volcano, and wellness: Papagayo, Guanacaste, Arenal, Manuel Antonio, and Osa Peninsula for the ultimate nature-and-luxury stay.

## 5-Star / Eco-Luxury Resorts

- **Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica at Peninsula Papagayo** — Guanacaste; two beaches, golf, spa; reference resort.
- **Nayara Springs** — Arenal; adults-only, thermal pools, rainforest; intimate.
- **Nayara Tented Camp** — Arenal; luxury tents, volcano view, pools; safari-luxe.
- **Andaz Costa Rica Resort at Peninsula Papagayo** — Guanacaste; bay, design, beach; Hyatt.
- **W Costa Rica - Reserva Conchal** — Guanacaste; beach, pool, LIV; scene and nature.
- **Tabacón Thermal Resort & Spa** — Arenal; hot springs, gardens, spa; thermal focus.
- **Los Sueños Marriott Ocean & Golf Resort** — Central Pacific; marina, golf, beach.
- **Pacuare Lodge** — Pacuare River; adventure, raft-in, rainforest; eco-luxury.
- **Lapa Rios** — Osa Peninsula; rainforest, wildlife, bungalows; National Geographic lodge.
- **Arenas del Mar** — Manuel Antonio; beach, rainforest, sustainability; eco.
- **Senda Monteverde** — Monteverde; cloud forest, small, immersive.
- **Casa Chameleon Las Catalinas** — Guanacaste; adults-only, hillside, pool; boutique.
- **The Springs Resort & Spa** — Arenal; hot springs, volcano views, family; thermal.
- **Nantipa** — Santa Teresa; beach, boho-luxe, Pacific; surf and relax.

## Live the life

- **Beach (Papagayo / Guanacaste):** Four Seasons, Andaz, W Costa Rica; ocean, golf, resort.
- **Rainforest / volcano (Arenal):** Nayara Springs, Nayara Tented Camp, Tabacón, The Springs; hot springs, wildlife.
- **Pacific (Manuel Antonio, Osa, Santa Teresa):** Arenas del Mar, Lapa Rios, Nantipa; beach, nature, surf.
- **Adventure:** Pacuare Lodge; raft-in, secluded.
