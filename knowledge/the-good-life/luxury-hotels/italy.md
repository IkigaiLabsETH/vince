# Italy — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life (Beyond Milan)

## Overview

Italy 5-star hotels span Rome, Florence, Venice, Lake Como, Amalfi Coast, and beyond: historic palazzi, lakeside villas, and world-class dining. For **Milan**, see **milan.md**.

## Rome

- **Hotel Hassler Roma** — Trinità dei Monti; Spanish Steps, rooftop, Imàgo (1*).
- **Palazzo Manfredi** — Oppian Hill; Colosseum views, Aroma (1*).
- **J.K. Place Roma** — Campo Marzio; boutique, design, central.
- **Hotel de la Ville** — Spanish Steps; Rocco Forte, rooftop bar.
- **The St. Regis Rome** — Via Veneto; historic, Butler service.

## Florence

- **Four Seasons Hotel Firenze** — Historic centre; palazzo and garden, Il Palagio.
- **Portrait Firenze** — Lungarno; Ferragamo, river views, intimate.
- **Villa Cora** — Oltrarno; hill, garden, pool, Belle Époque.
- **St. Regis Florence** — Piazza Ognissanti; river, frescoes.

## Venice

- **Hotel Cipriani, A Belmond Hotel** — Giudecca; lagoon, pool, gardens; iconic.
- **Gritti Palace** — Grand Canal; Rocco Forte, Club del Doge terrace.
- **Aman Venice** — Grand Canal; palazzo, Aman service.
- **Bauer Palazzo** — San Marco; historic, De Pisis dining.
- **Belmond Hotel Splendido** — Portofino (Liguria); harbour, hillside.

## Lake Como

- **Grand Hotel Tremezzo** — Tremezzo; lake, pool on water, T Pizza, central.
- **Villa d'Este** — Cernobbio; legendary, gardens, lake.
- **Mandarin Oriental, Lago di Como** — Blevio; villa, spa, lake.
- **Il Sereno** — Torno; contemporary, design, lake.

## Amalfi Coast

- **Il San Pietro di Positano** — Positano; cliff, lift to beach, dining.
- **Le Sirenuse** — Positano; views, La Sponda, pool.
- **Belmond Hotel Caruso** — Ravello; infinity pool, gardens, views.
- **Hotel Santa Caterina** — Amalfi; cliff, sea, classic.

## Live the life

- **City (Rome, Florence, Venice):** Hassler, Four Seasons Florence, Cipriani; culture, dining.
- **Lakes / coast:** Tremezzo, Villa d'Este, Il San Pietro; views, relaxation.
