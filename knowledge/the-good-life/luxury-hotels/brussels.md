# Brussels — Best 5-Star Hotels to Live the Life

## Overview

Brussels 5-star hotels sit near Grand Place, EU quarter, and Sablon: classic grandeur, art nouveau, and top dining for a refined stay.

## 5-Star Hotels

- **Hotel Amigo** — Grand Place; Rocco Forte, cobbled square, refined.
- **Steigenberger Wiltcher's** — Avenue Louise; classic, spa, central.
- **Sofitel Brussels Europe** — EU quarter; contemporary, business and leisure.
- **Juliana Hotel Brussels** — Central; design, restaurant, bar.
- **The Hotel Brussels** — Boulevard de Waterloo; views, contemporary.
- **Rocco Forte Hotel Amigo** — Grand Place; see Hotel Amigo.

## Live the life

- **Grand Place / centre:** Amigo; historic heart, dining, bars.
- **EU / Louise:** Steigenberger, Sofitel; polished, well located.
