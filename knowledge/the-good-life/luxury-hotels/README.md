## Hotel Selection Criteria

1. **Character**: Unique properties with personality over cookie-cutter luxury
2. **Gastronomy**: On-site or proximity to Michelin-starred dining
3. **Wine**: Access to notable wine regions and experiences
4. **Service**: Personalized, anticipatory service
5. **Location**: Either stunning natural setting or prime urban location
6. **Design**: Thoughtful architecture and interiors
