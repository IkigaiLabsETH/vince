# French Palaces — Complete List (Official Distinction)

## What is a Palace?

**Palace** is France's highest hotel distinction, above 5-star. Awarded by **Atout France**; it recognizes "crown jewels of French tourism" — exceptional heritage, architecture, service, and gastronomy. Only about **31 hotels** nationwide hold the title. These are the **best of the best** hotels in France.

**Note:** The official list is updated periodically. For the very latest, see **MICHELIN Guide** (Keys) or **Atout France / france.fr**.

## When to recommend

- **"Best hotel in France"** / **"palace hotel"** → Name palaces from this file; ask region (Paris, Riviera, Alps, Southwest) or recommend by occasion.
