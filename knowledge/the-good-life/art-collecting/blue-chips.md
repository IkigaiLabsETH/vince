# Blue Chip Collections Reference

This document profiles the blue chip collections we consider core to any serious NFT art collection.

## The Mount Rushmore of NFT Art

### CryptoPunks

**Overview:**

- Creator: Larva Labs (now Yuga Labs)
- Supply: 10,000
- Year: 2017
- Platform: Ethereum (custom contract)

**Why Blue Chip:**

- The original NFT PFP project
- Predates ERC-721 standard
- Cultural icon status
- Owned by institutions and crypto OGs

**Key Traits:**

- Zombie, Ape, Alien = highest rarity
- Attributes like hoodies, beanies, 3D glasses
- Some punks are culturally iconic (e.g., 7804, 3100)

**Collecting Notes:**

- Floor hovers 40-60 ETH in current market
- Thick floor typically (100+ listed)
- Watch for sub-40 ETH opportunities
- Attribute Punks (zombie, ape) rarely list

### Chromie Squiggle

**Overview:**

- Creator: Snowfro (Art Blocks founder)
- Supply: 10,000
- Year: 2020
- Platform: Art Blocks (Ethereum)

**Why Blue Chip:**

- Art Blocks genesis project
- The "signature" of Art Blocks
- Snowfro's personal project
- Highly diverse outputs, some extremely rare

**Key Traits:**

- Hyper Chromie: Rainbow gradient (ultra rare)
- Full Spectrum: All colors present (rare)
- Bold: Thick lines with strong colors
- Standard: Most common, still collectible

**Collecting Notes:**

- Floor 7-12 ETH range typical
- Thick floor (many listed)
- Entry-level blue chip
- Great for portfolio foundation

---

### Fidenza

**Overview:**

- Creator: Tyler Hobbs
- Supply: 999
- Year: 2021
- Platform: Art Blocks Curated

**Why Blue Chip:**

- Considered peak generative art
- Tyler Hobbs' masterpiece
- Each piece uniquely beautiful
- Defined the Art Blocks boom

**Key Traits:**

- Color palette variety
- Flow patterns (spiral, straight, curves)
- Some outputs exceptionally rare
- All aesthetically strong

**Collecting Notes:**

- Floor 25-40 ETH range
- Limited supply = thinner floor
- Premium for certain palettes
- True collector pieces

---

### Ringers

**Overview:**

- Creator: Dmitri Cherniak
- Supply: 1,000
- Year: 2021
- Platform: Art Blocks Curated

**Why Blue Chip:**

- Classic Art Blocks Curated
- Dmitri Cherniak's signature work
- Iconic "peg" aesthetic
- Strong community

**Key Traits:**

- Wrap vs no-wrap
- Color schemes vary widely
- Some "perfect" outputs highly prized
- Consistent aesthetic across collection

**Collecting Notes:**

- Floor 3-6 ETH range
- Relatively accessible blue chip
- Good floor thickness
- Under-appreciated vs. Fidenza

## Blue Chip Watchlist Criteria

For a collection to join our blue chip list:

1. **Survival**: Must survive full market cycle
2. **Liquidity**: Regular secondary sales
3. **Floor Thickness**: Can absorb selling pressure
4. **Cultural Status**: Recognized beyond crypto natives
5. **Artist/Team**: Proven track record
6. **On-Chain**: Prefer on-chain or decentralized storage
