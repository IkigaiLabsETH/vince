## Red Flags to Monitor

When evaluating emerging artists, watch for:

1. **Oversupply**: Artist releasing too frequently
2. **Style Drift**: Changing aesthetic radically
3. **Hype Over Substance**: More marketing than art
4. **No Community**: Silence between drops
5. **Price Decline**: Consistent secondary weakness

## Evaluation Criteria

Before adding an emerging artist:

| Criterion | Question | Weight |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| Aesthetic | Do we genuinely love the work? | 30% |
| Consistency | Does artist have coherent vision? | 20% |
| Community | Is there genuine collector base? | 15% |
| Scarcity | Limited editions or oversupply? | 15% |
| History | Track record in web3? | 10% |
| Pricing | Reasonable relative to peers? | 10% |
