Why Covered Calls Are Particularly Effective on Tesla Right Now

A covered call involves owning at least 100 shares of the underlying stock and selling one call option contract against those shares. You collect the premium immediately. If the stock price remains below the strike price at expiration, the option expires worthless, you keep the premium, and you retain your shares. If the stock rises above the strike and the option is exercised, your shares are called away at the strike price‚Äîbut you still keep the premium, effectively selling the shares at a higher realized price.
