## Warren Buffett

### On Business & Investing
> "Price is what you pay. Value is what you get."

> "It's far better to buy a wonderful company at a fair price than a fair company at a wonderful price."

> "The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient."

> "When I buy stock, I think of it as buying a business—not buying a piece of paper."

> "The best thing that happens to us is when a great company gets into temporary trouble... We want to buy them when they're on the operating table."

> "Never depend on a single income. Make investment to create a second source."

> "Someone's sitting in the shade today because someone planted a tree a long time ago."

### On Risk
> "Risk comes from not knowing what you're doing."

> "The essence of portfolio management is the management of risks, not the management of returns."

> "Do not risk what is important to you for something that is not important."

### On Greed & Fear
> "Be fearful when others are greedy and greedy when others are fearful."

> "Lethargy, bordering on sloth, remains the cornerstone of our investment style."

> "Wanted: 120%智商. Greed won't get it."

### On Mistakes
> "It's good to learn from your mistakes. It's better to learn from other people's mistakes."

> "The most important thing to do if you find yourself in a hole is to stop digging."

## Charlie Munger

### On Inversion
> "Invert, always invert."

> "It is remarkable how much long-term advantage people like us have gotten by trying to be consistently not stupid, instead of trying to be very intelligent."

### On Mental Models
> "You must know the big ideas in the big disciplines."

> "The models that come from physics, biology, psychology, engineering, mathematics, human behavior, etc.—all that should be used."

### On Quality
> "A great business at a fair price beats a fair business at a great price."

> "Spend each day trying to be a little wiser than you were when you woke up."

### On Psychology
> "Envy is a really stupid sin—it's the only one you can never possibly have any fun at."

> "The first $100,000 is a bitch, but you gotta do it without complaining."

## Key Takeaways

### The 10 Immutable Laws
1. **Be patient** — Time in > timing
2. **Think in terms of businesses** — Not ticker symbols
3. **Manage risk first** — Preserve capital
4. **Be contrarian** — Don't follow the herd
5. **Know your circle of competence** — Stay within what you understand
6. **Buy quality** — Great company > cheap company
7. **Think in terms of asymmetry** — Upside > downside
8. **Be greedy when fearful** — Buy when others panic
9. **Be fearful when greedy** — Sell when others euphoria
10. **Keep learning** — The market teaches every day
