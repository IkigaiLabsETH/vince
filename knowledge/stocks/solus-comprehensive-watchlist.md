### How Stocks Trade on Hyperliquid

**Perpetual Futures (Perps)**
- Hyperliquid offers **perpetual futures** on many stocks ‚Äî NOT the actual stocks
- You get synthetic price exposure, not ownership
- Cash-settled, no dividend rights
- High leverage available (up to 50x+)

**Spot Trading**
- Limited spot trading for certain assets
- Mostly tokens, not individual stocks

### Projects Enabling Real Stock Trading on Hyperliquid

#### Ventuals (ventuals.xyz)
- **What:** Real-world asset (RWA) platform bringing stocks on-chain
- **How:** Tokenized equities as ERC-20s, fully backed by real shares
- **Status:** Integrates with Hyperliquid ecosystem
- **Benefit:** Actual stock ownership, dividends, voting rights
- **Note:** Verify current status and supported assets

#### Other RWA Platforms to Watch
- **Mountain Protocol** ‚Äî USDR, tokenized treasuries
- **Ondo** ‚Äî USDY, tokenized US Treasuries
