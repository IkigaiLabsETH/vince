# AI Infrastructure Sector Deep Dive

*Research: 2026-02-19*

---

## Sector Overview

AI Infrastructure is the backbone of the AI revolution. These companies provide the compute, storage, and networking needed to train and run AI models.

**Key Themes:**
- Data center buildout
- GPU cloud services
- AI-specific infrastructure
- Edge computing

## NBIS — Nebius Group

### Company
- **Ticker:** NBIS
- **Sector:** AI Infrastructure / Data Centers
- **Market Cap:** ~$5B
- **HQ:** Netherlands

### What They Do
European AI infrastructure company. Ex-Yandex team building data centers for AI training/inference across Europe.

### Why It Matters
- Europe-focused AI compute (less US competition)
- Experienced team from Yandex
- Growing demand for non-US data residency

### Catalysts
- European AI adoption
- Data center expansions
- Enterprise contracts

### Risks
- Competition from US hyperscalers
- Capital intensity
- Execution risk

## Investment Thesis

### Bull Case
- AI adoption continues accelerating
- Data center demand outpaces supply
- Edge computing growth
- European expansion (NBIS)

### Bear Case
- Overbuilding
- Power constraints
- Competition from hyperscalers
- Interest rates impact REITs

### Key Metrics to Watch
- Occupancy rates (REITs)
- Bookings/Backlog
- Power utilization
- Customer concentration
