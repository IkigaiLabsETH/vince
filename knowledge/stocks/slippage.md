The On-Chain Liquidity Mirage\*\*

Tokenized TSLA liquidity? Solid for retail apes, low slippage on small size. But “size” meaning seven figs? Lol, you’d move the market like a whale dumping alts in a bear. It’s scary for size, fam. Straight up.
