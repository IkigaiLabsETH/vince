## NVDA — NVIDIA

### Company
- **Ticker:** NVDA
- **Sector:** Semiconductors / AI
- **Market Cap:** ~$3T
- **HQ:** USA

### What They Do

### Why It's Here

- Data center dominance
- Ecosystem lock-in
- Robotics (Isaac, Jetson)

- B100/H200 ramp
- Data center growth

- Networking (Mellanox)

- Competition (AMD, Intel, custom)
- Export controls (China)
- Valuation
- Supply
