# Building a 24/7 AI Trading Assistant: From Setup to Results

**Source**: Direct Input - January 29, 2025

## Summary

This is a detailed case study of implementing a 24/7 AI trading assistant called "Juno" using ClawdBot on a dedicated Mac Mini. The author documents the transition from skeptical setup to productive results within 48 hours, emphasizing the mental shift from being a worker to being a director of AI systems. The core insight: successful AI implementation requires extensive context-setting, proper memory systems, and treating the AI as a proactive employee rather than a reactive tool. The setup involves four critical prompts that solve memory persistence, create compounding knowledge systems, and enable overnight autonomous work.

The framework represents a fundamental shift in trading workflow - from grinding through tasks to focusing on discovery and guidance while AI handles execution. This approach creates compounding advantages through persistent memory, automated research, and 24/7 productivity that builds on itself over time.

## Key Points

• **Mental Shift Required**: Transition from being the executor to being the director - 95% of time spent on great prompts and context, 5% on execution
• **Memory is Everything**: Out-of-box AI forgets everything; proper memory systems are non-negotiable for compounding knowledge
• **Context Density Matters**: The AI needs your entire trading philosophy, playbooks, and belief systems to be effective - "like teaching a baby god how to trade"
• **Dedicated Hardware Essential**: Running AI assistant on trading machine creates unacceptable risk; $599 Mac Mini provides isolated environment
• **Four Critical Prompts**: Memory flush, three-layer memory system, conversation preservation, and overnight autonomous work
• **Read-Only Access Strategy**: Connect to trading data with read permissions only - AI can analyze 607 trades across 29 databases without risk
• **Proactive vs Reactive**: Best results come from AI that finds problems and solutions independently, not waiting for instructions
• **Compound Time Advantage**: Early adopters get structural edge; in 2-3 years non-adapted traders will be "blown out"
• **Speed of Learning**: Day 1 learning, Day 2 producing, Hour 48 applying philosophy better than the human operator
• **Total Cost Structure**: $599 upfront + $200/month Claude Max - "less than one good trading day"
