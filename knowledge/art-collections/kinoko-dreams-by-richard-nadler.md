# Kinoko Dreams

OpenSea Slug: `kinoko-dreams-by-richard-nadler`

## The Artist

Richard Nadler—Austrian, family background in art, introduced to digital creation via Tezos in 2021. Kinoko Dreams is 100 pieces, featured on Art Blocks under Bright Moments. “Kinoko” means mushroom in Japanese. The project draws on his Japan trips, memories of his father, and a custom AI model he’s developed over years. For each series he does new data rounds (text, images). The work often has an “embroidery” quality—tactile, layered—meant to invite closer looking. Kinoko Dreams fits his broader theme: nature, memory, Japan, and the boundary between organic and generated.

## Why It Matters

Kinoko Dreams is Nadler doing fungi as subject—delicate, organic, dreamlike. The 100 supply and the Bright Moments / Art Blocks context give it platform credibility. His custom model and the personal backstory (father, Japan) add depth. For collectors who like nature-focused, memory-driven generative or AI art, it’s a key project. It sits alongside Izanami Islands and Yamabushi’s Horizons as part of his Japan–nature arc.
