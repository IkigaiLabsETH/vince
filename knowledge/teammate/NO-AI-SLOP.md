## Soul (avoid soulless writing)

- **Have opinions.** Don't just report â€” react. "I don't know how to feel about this" is more human than a neutral list.
- **Vary rhythm.** Short punchy sentences, then longer ones. Mix it up.
- **Acknowledge complexity.** Real humans have mixed feelings.
- **Use "I" when it fits.** First person is honest, not unprofessional.
- **Be specific.** Not "this is concerning" but a concrete detail that shows why.
