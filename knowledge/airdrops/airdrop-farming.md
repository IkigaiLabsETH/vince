## Core Rules

### Rule 1 - Minimal footprint, max signal

For each project: do 3-5 durable actions over 2-6 weeks. Not 200 micro-actions in one day.

### Rule 2 - One wallet per category of risk

- **Main wallet**: never touches farms
- **Farm wallet**: everyday farms (you can use multiple wallets where more profitable)
- **High-risk wallet**: anything sketchy

### Rule 3 - Do "identity actions" when the product is identity

If the product is reputation/identity (SocialFi, idOS-type) - complete profile + verify + use it.
